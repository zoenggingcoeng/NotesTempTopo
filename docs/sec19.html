19.1 Function and sequence spaces. | 函数与序列空间

19.1.0.1 Definition: For sets \(X\) and \(Y\), the power set denoted by \(Y^X\) is the set \(\text{Map}(X; Y)\) of maps \(f: X \to Y\). For any \(p \in X\), the evaluation map at \(p\) is \[\text{ev}_p: Y^X \to Y, \quad \text{ev}_p(f) = f(p), \qquad (258)\] which is nothing but \(\text{pr}_p\) written in a more comprehensible way.
[点击展开翻译] 19.1.0.1 定义： 对于集合 \(X\) 和 \(Y\)，记为 \(Y^X\) 的幂集是映射 \(f: X \to Y\) 的集合 \(\text{Map}(X; Y)\)。对于任意 \(p \in X\)，在 \(p\) 处的赋值映射 (evaluation map) 是 \[\text{ev}_p: Y^X \to Y, \quad \text{ev}_p(f) = f(p), \qquad (258)\] 这只不过是 \(\text{pr}_p\) 用一种更容易理解的方式写出来而已。
19.1.0.2 Definition: The uniform topology \(\mathcal{T}_{\text{uni}}\) on \(\mathbb{R}^I\) is the topology generated by the base \[\mathcal{S}_{\text{uni}} = \left\{ \left\{ f \in \mathbb{R}^I : \sup_{i \in I}(f(i) - f_0(i)) < \varepsilon \right\} : f_0 \in \mathbb{R}^I, \varepsilon > 0 \right\}. \qquad (259)\]
[点击展开翻译] 19.1.0.2 定义： \(\mathbb{R}^I\) 上的一致拓扑 (uniform topology) \(\mathcal{T}_{\text{uni}}\) 是由以下基生成的拓扑： \[\mathcal{S}_{\text{uni}} = \left\{ \left\{ f \in \mathbb{R}^I : \sup_{i \in I}(f(i) - f_0(i)) < \varepsilon \right\} : f_0 \in \mathbb{R}^I, \varepsilon > 0 \right\}. \qquad (259)\]

19.1.0.3 Theorem: Let \((f_n)_{n \in \mathbb{N}}\) be a sequence in \(Y^X\), that is, a sequence of maps \(X \to Y\), and let \(f \in Y^X\). Then \(f_n \to f\) as \(n \to \infty\)

  1. in the product topology \(\mathcal{T}_{\text{prod}}\) iff it converges pointwisely;
  2. in the box topology \(\mathcal{T}_{\text{box}}\) when \(X\) is Hausdorff iff it has a subsequence eventually constant for all but finitely many points and in a uniform way; or
  3. in the uniform topology \(\mathcal{T}_{\text{uni}}\) when \(Y = \mathbb{R}\) iff it converges uniformly.
[点击展开翻译]

19.1.0.3 定理： 设 \((f_n)_{n \in \mathbb{N}}\) 是 \(Y^X\) 中的一个序列，即一系列映射 \(X \to Y\)，且设 \(f \in Y^X\)。那么当 \(n \to \infty\) 时 \(f_n \to f\)：

  1. 在乘积拓扑 \(\mathcal{T}_{\text{prod}}\) 中，当且仅当它逐点收敛；
  2. 在盒子拓扑 \(\mathcal{T}_{\text{box}}\) 中（当 \(X\) 是 Hausdorff 空间时），当且仅当它有一个子序列除了有限多个点外最终是常数，并且是以一致的方式；或者
  3. 在一致拓扑 \(\mathcal{T}_{\text{uni}}\) 中（当 \(Y = \mathbb{R}\) 时），当且仅当它一致收敛。
19.1.0.4 Remark: Recall in linear algebra, for a family \(\{X_i\}_{i \in I}\) of linear spaces, we define their direct sum as \[\bigoplus_{i \in I} X_i = \left\{ p \in \prod_{i \in I} X_i : (\exists J \subseteq I)(|J| < \infty) \wedge (\forall i \in I \setminus J)(p(i) = 0) \right\} \qquad (260)\] which is a proper subset of their direct product \(\prod_{i \in I} X_i\), which coincides with their cartesian product. We use \(\mathbb{R}^\infty\) to denote the direct sum of \(\mathbb{N}\)-copies of \(\mathbb{R}\) while \(\mathbb{R}^\omega\) for the direct product of \(\mathbb{N}\)-copies of \(\mathbb{R}\). Then \(\mathbb{R}^\omega\) coincides with the \(\mathbb{R}^{\mathbb{N}}\) notation.
[点击展开翻译] 19.1.0.4 备注： 回顾线性代数中，对于线性空间族 \(\{X_i\}_{i \in I}\)，我们将它们的直和 (direct sum) 定义为 \[\bigoplus_{i \in I} X_i = \left\{ p \in \prod_{i \in I} X_i : (\exists J \subseteq I)(|J| < \infty) \wedge (\forall i \in I \setminus J)(p(i) = 0) \right\} \qquad (260)\] 它是它们的直积 (direct product) \(\prod_{i \in I} X_i\) 的真子集，其中直积与它们的笛卡尔积一致。 我们使用 \(\mathbb{R}^\infty\) 来表示 \(\mathbb{R}\) 的 \(\mathbb{N}\) 个副本的直和，而用 \(\mathbb{R}^\omega\) 表示 \(\mathbb{R}\) 的 \(\mathbb{N}\) 个副本的直积。那么 \(\mathbb{R}^\omega\) 与 \(\mathbb{R}^{\mathbb{N}}\) 记号一致。
19.1.0.5 Proposition: For a set \(X\), the power set \(X^{\mathbb{N}}\) is the set of sequences on \(X\).
[点击展开翻译] 19.1.0.5 命题： 对于集合 \(X\)，幂集 \(X^{\mathbb{N}}\) 是 \(X\) 上序列的集合。

19.1.0.6 Remark: Recall in functional analysis we learnt on \(\mathbb{R}\) spaces of

  1. sequences \(\mathbb{R}^\omega\),
  2. eventually zero sequences \(c_c(\mathbb{R})\),
  3. summable sequences \(l^1(\mathbb{R})\),
  4. square summable sequences \(l^2(\mathbb{R})\),
  5. sequences converge to zero \(c_0(\mathbb{R})\),
  6. bounded sequences \(l^\infty(\mathbb{R})\). We have \[\mathbb{R}^\infty = c_c(\mathbb{R}) \subset l^1(\mathbb{R}) \subset l^2(\mathbb{R}) \subset c_0(\mathbb{R}) \subset l^\infty(\mathbb{R}) \subset \mathbb{R}^\omega. \qquad (261)\]
[点击展开翻译]

19.1.0.6 备注： 回顾在泛函分析中，我们学习了关于 \(\mathbb{R}\) 的以下空间：

  1. 序列 \(\mathbb{R}^\omega\)，
  2. 最终为零的序列 \(c_c(\mathbb{R})\)，
  3. 可和序列 \(l^1(\mathbb{R})\)，
  4. 平方可和序列 \(l^2(\mathbb{R})\)，
  5. 收敛到零的序列 \(c_0(\mathbb{R})\)，
  6. 有界序列 \(l^\infty(\mathbb{R})\)。 我们有 \[\mathbb{R}^\infty = c_c(\mathbb{R}) \subset l^1(\mathbb{R}) \subset l^2(\mathbb{R}) \subset c_0(\mathbb{R}) \subset l^\infty(\mathbb{R}) \subset \mathbb{R}^\omega. \qquad (261)\]
19.1.0.7 Theorem: Let \(\left(x^{(i)}\right)_{i \in \mathbb{N}}\) be a sequence in \(\mathbb{R}^\omega\) and for every \(i \in \mathbb{N}\), write \(x^{(i)} = \left(x_n^{(i)}\right)_{n \in \mathbb{N}}\). Then \(\left(x^{(i)}\right)_{i \in \mathbb{N}}\) converges to \(0^\omega\) in the box topology iff it converges to \(0^\omega\) in the product topology and there are \(I, N \in \mathbb{N}\) such that if \(i > I\) and \(n > N\) then \(x_n^{(i)} = 0\).
[点击展开翻译] 19.1.0.7 定理： 设 \(\left(x^{(i)}\right)_{i \in \mathbb{N}}\) 是 \(\mathbb{R}^\omega\) 中的一个序列，并且对于每个 \(i \in \mathbb{N}\)，记 \(x^{(i)} = \left(x_n^{(i)}\right)_{n \in \mathbb{N}}\)。那么 \(\left(x^{(i)}\right)_{i \in \mathbb{N}}\) 在盒子拓扑中收敛到 \(0^\omega\) 当且仅当它在乘积拓扑中收敛到 \(0^\omega\) 并且存在 \(I, N \in \mathbb{N}\) 使得如果 \(i > I\) 且 \(n > N\)，则有 \(x_n^{(i)} = 0\)。

19.2 Compact-open topology | 紧开拓扑

19.2.1 Definition of the compact-open topology | 紧开拓扑的定义

The compact-open topology is one of the commonly used topologies on function spaces, and is applied in homotopy theory and functional analysis. It was introduced by Ralph Fox in 1945.

[点击展开翻译] 紧开拓扑是函数空间上最常用的拓扑之一，应用于同伦论和泛函分析。它由 Ralph Fox 于 1945 年引入。

19.2.1.1 Definition: Let \(X\) and \(Y\) be two topological spaces. The compact-open topology \(\mathcal{T}_{\text{co}}\) on \(\mathrm{C}(X;Y)\) is the topology generated by the subbase

\[\mathcal{S} = \{V(K;U) : \text{compact } K \subseteq X, \text{open } U \subseteq Y\} \tag{262}\]

where

\[V(K;U) = \{f \in \mathrm{C}(X;Y) : f(K) \subseteq U\}. \tag{263}\]

[点击展开翻译]

设 \(X\) 与 \(Y\) 是两个拓扑空间。\(\mathrm{C}(X;Y)\) 上的紧开拓扑 \(\mathcal{T}_{\text{co}}\) 是由以下子基生成的拓扑：

\[\mathcal{S} = \{V(K;U) : \text{紧集 } K \subseteq X,\, \text{开集 } U \subseteq Y\}\]

其中

\[V(K;U) = \{f \in \mathrm{C}(X;Y) : f(K) \subseteq U\}.\]

A subbase for the open sets consists of the sets \(N(C,U)\) where \(C\) is any compact subset of \(Y\), \(U\) any open subset of \(Z\), and \(N(C,U)\) consists of all those continuous \(h: Y \to Z\) for which \(hC \subseteq U\).

[点击展开翻译] 开集的一个子基由集合 \(N(C,U)\) 构成，其中 \(C\) 是 \(Y\) 的任意紧子集，\(U\) 是 \(Z\) 的任意开子集，\(N(C,U)\) 由所有满足 \(hC \subseteq U\) 的连续映射 \(h: Y \to Z\) 组成。

19.2.1.2 Remark: We can write \(V(K;U) = \mathrm{C}(K;U) \times_{\mathrm{C}(K;Y)} \mathrm{C}(X;Y)\).

[点击展开翻译] 我们可以将 \(V(K;U)\) 写作 \(\mathrm{C}(K;U) \times_{\mathrm{C}(K;Y)} \mathrm{C}(X;Y)\)。

If the codomain of the functions under consideration has a uniform structure or a metric structure then the compact-open topology is the “topology of uniform convergence on compact sets.” That is to say, a sequence of functions converges in the compact-open topology precisely when it converges uniformly on every compact subset of the domain.

[点击展开翻译] 若所考虑函数的值域具有一致结构或度量结构，则紧开拓扑即为”紧集上一致收敛的拓扑”。也就是说，一列函数在紧开拓扑下收敛，当且仅当它在定义域的每个紧子集上一致收敛。

19.2.2 Properties of the compact-open topology | 紧开拓扑的性质

19.2.2.1 Proposition: The compact-open topology on \(\mathrm{C}(*,Y)\) where \(*\) is the point space agrees with the topology on \(Y\).

[点击展开翻译] \(\mathrm{C}(*,Y)\) 上的紧开拓扑（其中 \(*\) 为单点空间）与 \(Y\) 上的拓扑一致。

19.2.2.2 Proposition: If \(X\) is a discrete space, then \(\mathrm{C}(X;Y)\) is identified with the product space \(Y^X\).

[点击展开翻译] 若 \(X\) 是离散空间，则 \(\mathrm{C}(X;Y)\) 与乘积空间 \(Y^X\) 等同。

19.2.2.3 Proposition: If \(Y\) is \(\mathrm{T}_0\), \(\mathrm{T}_1\), Hausdorff, regular, or Tychonoff, then the compact-open topology satisfies the same separation axiom.

[点击展开翻译] 若 \(Y\) 满足 \(\mathrm{T}_0\)、\(\mathrm{T}_1\)、Hausdorff、正则或 Tychonoff 分离公理，则紧开拓扑也满足相同的分离公理。

19.2.2.4 Proposition: If \(X\) is Hausdorff and \(\mathcal{S}\) is a subbase for the topology on \(Y\), then

\[\mathcal{S} = \{V(K;U) : \text{compact } K \subseteq X,\, U \in \mathcal{S}\} \tag{264}\]

is a subbase for the compact-open topology on \(\mathrm{C}(X;Y)\).

[点击展开翻译]

若 \(X\) 是 Hausdorff 空间，且 \(\mathcal{S}\) 是 \(Y\) 上拓扑的一个子基，则

\[\mathcal{S} = \{V(K;U) : \text{紧集 } K \subseteq X,\, U \in \mathcal{S}\}\]

是 \(\mathrm{C}(X;Y)\) 上紧开拓扑的一个子基。

19.2.2.5 Proposition: If \((Y,d)\) is a metric space, then the compact-open topology is equal to the topology of compact convergence, in the sense that a sequence \(\{f_n\} \subseteq Y\) converges to \(f \in Y\) in the compact-open topology iff for every compact subset \(K\) of \(X\), \(\{f_n\}\) converges uniformly to \(f\) on \(K\).

[点击展开翻译] 若 \((Y,d)\) 是度量空间，则紧开拓扑等于紧收敛拓扑，即序列 \(\{f_n\} \subseteq Y\) 在紧开拓扑下收敛到 \(f \in Y\)，当且仅当对 \(X\) 的每个紧子集 \(K\)，\(\{f_n\}\) 在 \(K\) 上一致收敛到 \(f\)。

19.2.2.6 Proposition: If \(X\), \(Y\) and \(Z\) are topological spaces, with \(Y\) locally compact Hausdorff, then the composition map

\[\mathrm{C}(Y,Z) \times \mathrm{C}(X;Y) \to \mathrm{C}(X,Z), \quad (f,g) \mapsto f \circ g, \tag{265}\]

is continuous with the compact-open topology on all the function spaces and the product topology on the cartesian product.

[点击展开翻译]

若 \(X\)、\(Y\)、\(Z\) 是拓扑空间，且 \(Y\) 是局部紧 Hausdorff 空间，则复合映射

\[\mathrm{C}(Y,Z) \times \mathrm{C}(X;Y) \to \mathrm{C}(X,Z), \quad (f,g) \mapsto f \circ g\]

在所有函数空间上赋予紧开拓扑、笛卡尔积上赋予乘积拓扑时是连续的。

19.2.2.7 Proposition: If \(X\) is a locally compact Hausdorff space, then the evaluation map

\[\mathrm{ev}: \mathrm{C}(X;Y) \times X \to Y, \quad \mathrm{ev}(f,p) = f(p), \tag{266}\]

is continuous.

[点击展开翻译]

若 \(X\) 是局部紧 Hausdorff 空间，则赋值映射

\[\mathrm{ev}: \mathrm{C}(X;Y) \times X \to Y, \quad \mathrm{ev}(f,p) = f(p)\]

是连续的。

19.2.2.8 Proposition: If \(X\) is compact, and \((Y,d)\) is a metric space, then the compact-open topology on \(\mathrm{C}(X;Y)\) is induced by the metric \(e\) given by

\[e(f,g) = \sup\{d(f(p), g(p)) : p \in X\} \tag{267}\]

for \(f, g \in \mathrm{C}(X;Y)\).

[点击展开翻译]

若 \(X\) 是紧空间，\((Y,d)\) 是度量空间，则 \(\mathrm{C}(X;Y)\) 上的紧开拓扑由以下度量 \(e\) 诱导：

\[e(f,g) = \sup\{d(f(p), g(p)) : p \in X\}\]

其中 \(f, g \in \mathrm{C}(X;Y)\)。

19.2.3 Applications of the compact-open topology | 紧开拓扑的应用

The compact open topology can be used to topologize the following sets:

  • the loop space of \(X\) at \(p\) is \(\Omega_p(X) = \{f{:}[0,1] \to X : f(0) = f(1) = p\}\) (Definition 21.5.1.1),
  • the path space of \(X\) from \(p\) to \(q\) is \(\Omega_{p,q}(X) = \{f{:}[0,1] \to X : f(0) = p, f(1) = q\}\) (Definition 21.5.1.1), and
  • the path space of \(X\) from \(p\) is \(E_p(X) = \{f{:}[0,1] \to X : f(0) = p\}\).

In addition, there is a homotopy equivalence between the spaces \(\mathrm{C}(\Sigma X; Y) \simeq \mathrm{C}(X; \Omega Y)\). The topological spaces \(\mathrm{C}(X;Y)\) are useful in homotopy theory because they can be used to form a topological space and a model for the homotopy type of the set of homotopy classes of maps

\[\pi(X;Y) = \{[f]: X \to Y : f \text{ is a homotopy class}\}. \tag{268}\]

This is because \(\pi(X;Y)\) is the set of path components in \(\mathrm{C}(X;Y)\); that is, there is an isomorphism of sets \(\pi(X;Y) \to \mathrm{C}([0,1]; \mathrm{C}(X;Y))/{\sim}\), where \(\sim\) is the homotopy equivalence.

[点击展开翻译]

紧开拓扑可用于对以下集合赋予拓扑结构：

  • \(X\) 在 \(p\) 处的环路空间：\(\Omega_p(X) = \{f{:}[0,1] \to X : f(0) = f(1) = p\}\)（定义 21.5.1.1），
  • \(X\) 从 \(p\) 到 \(q\) 的路径空间：\(\Omega_{p,q}(X) = \{f{:}[0,1] \to X : f(0) = p,\, f(1) = q\}\)（定义 21.5.1.1），以及
  • \(X\) 从 \(p\) 出发的路径空间：\(E_p(X) = \{f{:}[0,1] \to X : f(0) = p\}\)。

此外，空间 \(\mathrm{C}(\Sigma X; Y) \simeq \mathrm{C}(X; \Omega Y)\) 之间存在同伦等价。拓扑空间 \(\mathrm{C}(X;Y)\) 在同伦论中十分有用，因为它们可以用来构成一个拓扑空间，并作为映射同伦类集合的同伦型模型：

\[\pi(X;Y) = \{[f]: X \to Y : f \text{ 是一个同伦类}\}.\]

这是因为 \(\pi(X;Y)\) 是 \(\mathrm{C}(X;Y)\) 中路径连通分支的集合；即存在集合同构 \(\pi(X;Y) \to \mathrm{C}([0,1]; \mathrm{C}(X;Y))/{\sim}\)，其中 \(\sim\) 为同伦等价关系。

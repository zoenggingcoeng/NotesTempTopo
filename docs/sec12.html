12.1.1 Separating points and subsets. | 分离点与子集。

How points or closed sets are separated by opens sets?

12.1.1.1 Definition: Two points \(p\) and \(q\) of a topological space \(X\) are

  • distinct iff they are different, or \(p \neq q\);
  • topologically distinguishable iff there exists a neighborhood of one point away from the other point, or \((\exists U \in \mathcal{N}_p)(q \notin U) \lor (\exists V \in \mathcal{N}_q)(p \notin V)\).
[点击展开翻译]

12.1.1.1 定义：拓扑空间 \(X\) 中的两点 \(p\) 和 \(q\) 是

  • 相异的 (distinct)，当且仅当它们不同，即 \(p \neq q\)；
  • 拓扑可辨识的 (topologically distinguishable)，当且仅当存在一个点的一个邻域不包含另一个点，即 \((\exists U \in \mathcal{N}_p)(q \notin U) \lor (\exists V \in \mathcal{N}_q)(p \notin V)\)。

12.1.1.2 Definition: Let \(A\) be a subset of a topological space \(X\). analogous to those of a point, \(U\) is an open neighborhood of \(A\) in \(X\) iff \(U\) is open and contains \(A\), and \(U\) is a neighborhood of \(A\) in \(X\) iff it contains an open neighborhood of \(A\). The collection of neighborhoods of \(A\) in \(X\) is denoted by \(\mathcal{N}_A(X)\) or \(\mathcal{N}_A\).

[点击展开翻译] 12.1.1.2 定义：设 \(A\) 为拓扑空间 \(X\) 的一个子集。类似于点的邻域，当且仅当 \(U\) 是开集且包含 \(A\) 时，\(U\) 是 \(A\) 在 \(X\) 中的一个开邻域 (open neighborhood)；当且仅当 \(U\) 包含 \(A\) 的一个开邻域时，\(U\) 是 \(A\) 在 \(X\) 中的一个邻域 (neighborhood)。\(A\) 在 \(X\) 中的邻域族记作 \(\mathcal{N}_A(X)\) 或 \(\mathcal{N}_A\)。

12.1.1.3 Definition: Two subsets \(A\) and \(B\) of a topological space \(X\) are

  • disjoint iff their intersection is empty, or \(A \cap B = \emptyset\);
  • topologically disjoint iff there exists a neighborhood of one set that is disjoint from the other set, or \((\exists U \in \mathcal{N}_A)(U \cap B = \emptyset) \lor (\exists V \in \mathcal{N}_B)(V \cap A = \emptyset)\);
  • separated iff each set has a neighborhood that is disjoint from the other set, or \((\exists U \in \mathcal{N}_A)(\exists V \in \mathcal{N}_B)(U \cap B = V \cap A = \emptyset)\);
  • separated by neighborhoods iff they have disjoint neighborhoods, or \((\exists U \in \mathcal{N}_A)(\exists V \in \mathcal{N}_B)(U \cap V = \emptyset)\);
  • separated by closed neighborhoods iff they have disjoint closed neighborhoods, or \((\exists U \in \mathcal{N}_A)(\exists V \in \mathcal{N}_B)(\overline{U} \cap \overline{V} = \emptyset)\);
  • separated by a function iff there exists a continuous real-valued function on the space that maps \(A\) to 0 and \(B\) to 1, or \((\exists \text{ continuous } f: X \to \mathbb{R})(A \subseteq f^{-1}(0) \land B \subseteq f^{-1}(1))\); or
  • precisely separated by a function iff there exists a continuous real-valued function on the space that maps precisely \(A\) to 0 and \(B\) to 1, or \((\exists \text{ continuous } f: X \to \mathbb{R})(A = f^{-1}(0) \land B = f^{-1}(1))\).
[点击展开翻译]

12.1.1.3 定义：拓扑空间 \(X\) 中的两个子集 \(A\) 和 \(B\) 是

  • 不相交的 (disjoint)，当且仅当它们的交集为空，即 \(A \cap B = \emptyset\)；
  • 拓扑不相交的 (topologically disjoint)，当且仅当存在一个集合的一个邻域与另一个集合不相交，即 \((\exists U \in \mathcal{N}_A)(U \cap B = \emptyset) \lor (\exists V \in \mathcal{N}_B)(V \cap A = \emptyset)\)；
  • 分离的 (separated)，当且仅当每个集合都有一个与另一个集合不相交的邻域，即 \((\exists U \in \mathcal{N}_A)(\exists V \in \mathcal{N}_B)(U \cap B = V \cap A = \emptyset)\)；
  • 由邻域分离的 (separated by neighborhoods)，当且仅当它们有不相交的邻域，即 \((\exists U \in \mathcal{N}_A)(\exists V \in \mathcal{N}_B)(U \cap V = \emptyset)\)；
  • 由闭邻域分离的 (separated by closed neighborhoods)，当且仅当它们有不相交的闭邻域，即 \((\exists U \in \mathcal{N}_A)(\exists V \in \mathcal{N}_B)(\overline{U} \cap \overline{V} = \emptyset)\)；
  • 由函数分离的 (separated by a function)，当且仅当空间上存在一个连续实值函数将 \(A\) 映射为 0 并将 \(B\) 映射为 1，即 \((\exists \text{ continuous } f: X \to \mathbb{R})(A \subseteq f^{-1}(0) \land B \subseteq f^{-1}(1))\)；或
  • 由函数精确分离的 (precisely separated by a function)，当且仅当空间上存在一个连续实值函数精确地将 \(A\) 映射为 0 并将 \(B\) 映射为 1，即 \((\exists \text{ continuous } f: X \to \mathbb{R})(A = f^{-1}(0) \land B = f^{-1}(1))\)。

12.1.1.4 Proposition: For two subsets of a topological space, being precisely separated by a function \(\implies\) separated by a function \(\implies\) separated by closed neighborhoods \(\implies\) separated by neighborhoods \(\implies\) separated \(\implies\) topologically disjoint \(\implies\) disjoint. For closed subsets, being separated \(\iff\) topologically disjoint \(\iff\) disjoint. For a closed set and a point, being topologically disjoint \(\iff\) disjoint. For two points, the root “tinct” means the same as “join”, and are called distinct iff they are disjoint, or called topologically distinguishable iff they are topologically disjoint.

[点击展开翻译] 12.1.1.4 命题：对于拓扑空间的两个子集，由函数精确分离 \(\implies\) 由函数分离 \(\implies\) 由闭邻域分离 \(\implies\) 由邻域分离 \(\implies\) 分离的 \(\implies\) 拓扑不相交的 \(\implies\) 不相交的。对于闭子集，分离的 \(\iff\) 拓扑不相交的 \(\iff\) 不相交的。对于一个闭集和一个点，拓扑不相交的 \(\iff\) 不相交的。对于两点，词根 “tinct” 的含义与 “join” 相同，当且仅当它们不相交时称为相异的 (distinct)，或者当且仅当它们拓扑不相交时称为拓扑可辨识的 (topologically distinguishable)。

12.1.1.5 Proposition: Two subsets \(A\) and \(B\) of a topological space \(X\) are separated iff they are disjoint from the closure of each other, in the sense of \(A \cap \overline{B} = \overline{A} \cap B = \emptyset\).\(^4\) Therefore, for two subsets, \[\text{separated by closed neighborhoods} \implies \text{separated by neighborhoods} \implies \text{separated,}\] \[\text{separated by closed neighborhoods} \implies \text{closures are disjoint} \implies \text{separated,} \quad (174)\] \[\text{disjoint from each other's closure} \iff \text{separated.}\] However, neither being “separated by neighborhoods” or “closures are disjoint” implies the other.

[点击展开翻译] 12.1.1.5 命题：拓扑空间 \(X\) 的两个子集 \(A\) 和 \(B\) 是分离的，当且仅当它们与彼此的闭包都不相交，即 \(A \cap \overline{B} = \overline{A} \cap B = \emptyset\)。\(^4\) 因此，对于两个子集， \[\text{由闭邻域分离} \implies \text{由邻域分离} \implies \text{分离的，}\] \[\text{由闭邻域分离} \implies \text{闭包不相交} \implies \text{分离的，} \quad (174)\] \[\text{与彼此的闭包不相交} \iff \text{分离的。}\] 然而，“由邻域分离”和“闭包不相交”两者均不能相互推导。

Proof: Let \(A = [0, 1[\) and \(B = ]1, 2]\) be subsets of \(\mathbb{R}\). They are separated by neighborhoods but closures are not disjoint. The other direction is an exercise. \(\blacksquare\)

12.1.2 Separation axioms. | 分离公理。

12.1.2.1 Definition: Let \(X\) be a topological space. We have the separation axioms.

  • \(\mathbf{T}_0\): any two distinct points in \(X\) are topologically distinguishable; or for any \(p,q \in X\), if \(p \neq q\) then either there is \(U \in \mathcal{N}_p\) such that \(q \notin U\), or there is \(V \in \mathcal{N}_q\) such that \(p \notin V\);
  • \(\mathbf{T}_1\): any two distinct points in \(X\) are separated; or for any \(p,q \in X\), if \(p \neq q\) then there are \(U \in \mathcal{N}_p\) and \(V \in \mathcal{N}_q\) such that \(p \notin V\) and \(q \notin U\);
  • \(\mathbf{T}_2\): any two distinct points in \(X\) are separated by neighborhoods; or for any \(p,q \in X\), if \(p \neq q\) then there are \(U \in \mathcal{N}_p\) and \(V \in \mathcal{N}_q\) such that \(U \cap V = \emptyset\);
  • \(\mathbf{T}_{2\frac{1}{2}}\): any two distinct points in \(X\) are separated by closed neighborhoods; or for any \(p,q \in X\), if \(p \neq q\) then there are \(U \in \mathcal{N}_p\) and \(V \in \mathcal{N}_q\) such that \(\overline{U} \cap \overline{V} = \emptyset\);
  • \(\mathbf{PT}_2\): any two distinct points in \(X\) are precisely separated by a function;
  • \(\mathbf{T}_3\): any disjoint point and closed set in \(X\) are separated by neighborhoods; or for any \(p \in X\) and closed \(B\) in \(X\), if \(p \notin B\) then there are \(U \in \mathcal{N}_p\) and \(V \in \mathcal{N}_B\) such that \(U \cap V = \emptyset\);
  • \(\mathbf{T}_{3\frac{1}{2}}\): any disjoint point and closed set in \(X\) are separated by a function; or for any \(p \in X\) and closed \(B\) in \(X\), if \(p \notin B\) then there is a continuous function \(f: X \to \mathbb{R}\) with \(f(A) = 0\) and \(f(B) = 1\);
  • \(\mathbf{T}_4\): any disjoint closed sets in \(X\) are separated by neighborhoods; or for any closed \(A\) and \(B\) in \(X\), if \(A \cap B = \emptyset\) then there are \(U \in \mathcal{N}_A\) and \(V \in \mathcal{N}_B\) such that \(U \cap V = \emptyset\);
  • \(\mathbf{T}_5\): any separated sets in \(X\) are separated by neighborhoods; or for any \(A,B \subseteq X\), if \(\overline{A} \cap B = A \cap \overline{B} = \emptyset\) then there are \(U \in \mathcal{N}_A\) and \(V \in \mathcal{N}_B\) such that \(U \cap V = \emptyset\);
  • \(\mathbf{PT}_4\): any disjoint closed sets in \(X\) are precisely separated by a function.

12.1.2.1 定义：设 \(X\) 为一个拓扑空间。我们有以下分离公理：

  • \(\mathbf{T}_0\)：\(X\) 中任意两个相异点是拓扑可辨识的；或者对于任意 \(p,q \in X\)，如果 \(p \neq q\)，则要么存在 \(U \in \mathcal{N}_p\) 使得 \(q \notin U\)，要么存在 \(V \in \mathcal{N}_q\) 使得 \(p \notin V\)；
  • \(\mathbf{T}_1\)：\(X\) 中任意两个相异点是分离的；或者对于任意 \(p,q \in X\)，如果 \(p \neq q\)，则存在 \(U \in \mathcal{N}_p\) 和 \(V \in \mathcal{N}_q\) 使得 \(p \notin V\) 且 \(q \notin U\)；
  • \(\mathbf{T}_2\)：\(X\) 中任意两个相异点由邻域分离；或者对于任意 \(p,q \in X\)，如果 \(p \neq q\)，则存在 \(U \in \mathcal{N}_p\) 和 \(V \in \mathcal{N}_q\) 使得 \(U \cap V = \emptyset\)；
  • \(\mathbf{T}_{2\frac{1}{2}}\)：\(X\) 中任意两个相异点由闭邻域分离；或者对于任意 \(p,q \in X\)，如果 \(p \neq q\)，则存在 \(U \in \mathcal{N}_p\) 和 \(V \in \mathcal{N}_q\) 使得 \(\overline{U} \cap \overline{V} = \emptyset\)；
  • \(\mathbf{PT}_2\)：\(X\) 中任意两个相异点由函数精确分离；
  • \(\mathbf{T}_3\)：\(X\) 中任意不相交的点与闭集由邻域分离；或者对于任意 \(p \in X\) 以及 \(X\) 中的闭集 \(B\)，如果 \(p \notin B\)，则存在 \(U \in \mathcal{N}_p\) 和 \(V \in \mathcal{N}_B\) 使得 \(U \cap V = \emptyset\)；
  • \(\mathbf{T}_{3\frac{1}{2}}\)：\(X\) 中任意不相交的点与闭集由函数分离；或者对于任意 \(p \in X\) 以及 \(X\) 中的闭集 \(B\)，如果 \(p \notin B\)，则存在连续函数 \(f: X \to \mathbb{R}\) 满足 \(f(A) = 0\) 和 \(f(B) = 1\)；
  • \(\mathbf{T}_4\)：\(X\) 中任意两个不相交的闭集由邻域分离；或者对于 \(X\) 中任意闭集 \(A\) 和 \(B\)，如果 \(A \cap B = \emptyset\)，则存在 \(U \in \mathcal{N}_A\) 和 \(V \in \mathcal{N}_B\) 使得 \(U \cap V = \emptyset\)；
  • \(\mathbf{T}_5\)：\(X\) 中任意分离集由邻域分离；或者对于任意 \(A,B \subseteq X\)，如果 \(\overline{A} \cap B = A \cap \overline{B} = \emptyset\)，则存在 \(U \in \mathcal{N}_A\) 和 \(V \in \mathcal{N}_B\) 使得 \(U \cap V = \emptyset\)；
  • \(\mathbf{PT}_4\)：\(X\) 中任意不相交的闭集由函数精确分离。

12.1.2.2 Definition: A topological space \(X\) is called

  • a \(\mathbf{T}_0\) space, or a Kolmogorov space, iff it satisfies the \(\mathbf{T}_0\) axiom;
  • a \(\mathbf{T}_1\) space, or a Fréchet space, iff it satisfies the \(\mathbf{T}_1\) axiom;
  • a \(\mathbf{T}_2\) space, or a Hausdorff space, iff it satisfies the \(\mathbf{T}_2\) axiom;
  • a \(\mathbf{T}_{2\frac{1}{2}}\) space, or a completely Hausdorff space, iff it satisfies the \(\mathbf{T}_{2\frac{1}{2}}\) axiom;
  • a perfectly \(\mathbf{T}_2\) space, or a perfectly Hausdorff space, iff it satisfies the \(\mathbf{PT}_2\) axiom;
  • a \(\mathbf{T}_3\) space, iff it satisfies the \(\mathbf{T}_3\) axiom;
  • a regular space, iff it satisfies the \(\mathbf{T}_0\) and \(\mathbf{T}_3\) axioms;
  • a \(\mathbf{T}_{3\frac{1}{2}}\) space, iff it satisfies the \(\mathbf{T}_{3\frac{1}{2}}\) axiom;
  • a completely regular space, or a Tychonoff space, iff it satisfies the \(\mathbf{T}_0\) and \(\mathbf{T}_{3\frac{1}{2}}\) axioms;
  • a \(\mathbf{T}_4\) space, iff it satisfies the \(\mathbf{T}_4\) axiom;
  • a normal space, iff it satisfies the \(\mathbf{T}_1\) and \(\mathbf{T}_4\) axioms;
  • a \(\mathbf{T}_5\) space, iff it satisfies the \(\mathbf{T}_5\) axiom.
  • a completely normal space, iff it satisfies the \(\mathbf{T}_1\) and \(\mathbf{T}_5\) axioms;
  • a perfectly \(\mathbf{T}_4\) space, iff it satisfies the \(\mathbf{PT}_4\) axiom.

12.1.3 Implication of separation axioms.

12.1.3.1 Remark: A higher \(\mathbf{T}_i\) axiom does not necessarily imply a lower one.

[点击展开翻译] 12.1.3.1 备注：较高的 \(\mathbf{T}_i\) 公理不一定蕴含较低的公理。

12.1.3.2 Proposition: \[ \begin{aligned} \mathbf{T}_5 &\implies \mathbf{T}_4 \\ \mathbf{T}_4 \land \mathbf{T}_3 &\implies \mathbf{T}_{3\frac{1}{2}} \\ \mathbf{T}_4 \land \mathbf{T}_1 &\implies \mathbf{T}_{3\frac{1}{2}} \implies \mathbf{T}_3 \\ \mathbf{T}_3 \land \mathbf{T}_2 &\implies \mathbf{T}_{2\frac{1}{2}} \\ \mathbf{T}_3 \land \mathbf{T}_0 &\implies \mathbf{T}_{2\frac{1}{2}} \implies \mathbf{T}_2 \implies \mathbf{T}_1 \implies \mathbf{T}_0 \end{aligned} \qquad (175) \]

[点击展开证明]

Proof: \(\mathbf{T}_5 \implies \mathbf{T}_4\). Any two disjoint closed sets are separated. \(\mathbf{T}_3 \land \mathbf{T}_0 \implies \mathbf{T}_2\). Let \(p, q \in X\) be two distinct points. By \(\mathbf{T}_0\), there is \(U \in \mathcal{T}\) containing exactly one of \(p\) and \(q\), and we assume without loss of generality that \(q \in U\) but \(p \notin U\). By \(\mathbf{T}_3\), there is \(V \in \mathcal{N}_q\) and \(W \in \mathcal{N}_{X \setminus U}\) with \(W \cap V = \emptyset\). In particular, \(W \in \mathcal{N}_p\) and \(X\) satisfies \(\mathbf{T}_2\). \(\mathbf{T}_3 \land \mathbf{T}_2 \implies \mathbf{T}_{2\frac{1}{2}}\). Let \(p, q \in X\) be two distinct points. By \(\mathbf{T}_2\), there are \(U \in \mathcal{N}_p\) and \(V \in \mathcal{N}_q\) such that \(U \cap V = \emptyset\). By \(\mathbf{T}_3\), there is \(U_1 \in \mathcal{N}_p\) and \(V_1 \in \mathcal{N}_q\) such that \(\overline{U_1} \subseteq U\) and \(\overline{V_1} \subseteq V\). Then \(\overline{U_1} \cap \overline{V_1} \subseteq U \cap V = \emptyset\) and \(X\) satisfies \(\mathbf{T}_{2\frac{1}{2}}\). \(\mathbf{T}_{2\frac{1}{2}} \implies \mathbf{T}_2 \implies \mathbf{T}_1 \implies \mathbf{T}_0\). Being separated by neighborhoods \(\implies\) separated \(\implies\) topologically disjoint. \(\blacksquare\)

12.3.1.1 Proposition: A topological space \(X\) is \(\mathrm{T}_1\)

  • iff every point in \(X\) is closed;
  • iff every point in \(X\) is the intersection of all its neighborhoods;
  • iff the topology on \(X\) is finer than the cofinite topology on \(X\);

[点击展开翻译]

  • 当且仅当 \(X\) 中的每个点都是闭的；
  • 当且仅当 \(X\) 中的每个点都是其所有邻域的交集；
  • 当且仅当 \(X\) 上的拓扑比 \(X\) 上的余有限拓扑更细；

[点击展开证明] Proof: The space \(X\) is \(\mathrm{T}_1\) iff for any \(p \in X\), the set \(X \setminus \{p\}\) is a neighborhood of every \(q \neq p\) which is equivalent to the openness of \(X \setminus \{p\}\) in \(X\). [点击展开翻译] 12.3.1.3 命题：如果 \(X\) 是一个 \(\mathrm{T}_1\) 空间且 \(p\) 是 \(A \subseteq X\) 的一个极限点，那么对于任意 \(U \in \mathcal{N}_p\)，都有 \(|U \cap A| = \infty\)。

12.4.1 Hausdorff spaces | 豪斯多夫空间

12.4.1.1 Proposition: A topological space \(X\) is \(\mathrm{T}_2\)

  • iff the diagonal map \(\Delta: X \to X \times X, p \mapsto (p,p)\) is a closed map;
  • iff the diagonal \(\Delta(X)\) is a closed subset of \(X \times X\);
  • iff every point in \(X\) is the intersection of all its closed neighborhoods.
[点击展开翻译]

12.4.1.1 命题：一个拓扑空间 \(X\) 是 \(\mathrm{T}_2\) 空间

  • 当且仅当对角映射 \(\Delta: X \to X \times X, p \mapsto (p,p)\) 是闭映射；
  • 当且仅当对角线 \(\Delta(X)\) 是 \(X \times X\) 的闭子集；
  • 当且仅当 \(X\) 中的每个点是其所有闭邻域的交集。
[点击展开证明] Proof: The space \(X\) is \(\mathrm{T}_2\) iff for any \((p, q) \notin \Delta(X)\) there are \(U \in \mathcal{N}_p\) and \(V \in \mathcal{N}_q\) such that \(U \cap V = \emptyset\) which is equivalent to \(\mathcal{N}_{(p,q)} \ni U \times V \subseteq \Delta(X)^\complement\), and therefore, it is equivalent to \(\Delta(X)^\complement\) being open in \(X \times X\). By the above lemma, the first and second items are equivalent. Let \(p, q \in X\) with \(p \neq q\). If \(X\) is \(\mathrm{T}_2\), then there are \(U \in \mathcal{N}_p\) and \(V \in \mathcal{N}_q\) such that \(U \cap V = \emptyset\), and then \(q \notin \overline{U} \supseteq \bigcap_{U \in \mathcal{N}_p} \overline{U}\). If \(\{p\} = \bigcap_{U \in \mathcal{N}_p} \overline{U}\), then there is \(U \in \mathcal{N}_p\) with \(q \notin \overline{U}\), and then \(\mathrm{Ext}(U) \in \mathcal{N}_q\) is disjoint from \(U\). \(\blacksquare\)

12.4.1.2 Theorem: In a \(\mathrm{T}_2\) space, the limit of a convergent sequence is unique.

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.1.2 定理：在 \(\mathrm{T}_2\) 空间中，收敛序列的极限是唯一的。
[点击展开证明] Proof: Let \((p_n)_{n \in \mathbb{N}}\) be a sequence in a \(\mathrm{T}_2\) space \(X\) that converges to both \(q_0\) and \(q_1\) in \(X\). Suppose for the sake of contradiction that \(q_0 \neq q_1\). By Hausdorffness, there are \(U_0 \in \mathcal{N}_{q_0}\) and \(U_1 \in \mathcal{N}_{q_1}\) such that \(U_0 \cap U_1 = \emptyset\). By convergence of the sequence, there is an \(N_i \in \mathbb{N}\) such that \(n \geq N_i \implies p_n \in U_i\), for \(i \in \{0, 1\}\). In particular, when \(n \geq \max(N_0, N_1)\), it is a contradiction that \(p_n \in U_0 \cap U_1 = \emptyset\). \(\blacksquare\)

12.4.1.3 Remark: Therefore, it is appropriate to write \(\lim_{n \to \infty} p_n = q\) only in a Hausdorff space, not in a general topological space.

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.1.3 备注：因此，只有在豪斯多夫空间中写 \(\lim_{n \to \infty} p_n = q\) 才是合适的，在一般拓扑空间中则不然。

12.4.1.4 Theorem: Let \(f_0, f_1: X \to Y\) be continuous maps with \(Y\) Hausdorff. Then the coincidence set \(C(f_0, f_1) = \{p \in X : f_0(p) = f_1(p)\}\) is closed.

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.1.4 定理：设 \(f_0, f_1: X \to Y\) 为连续映射，且 \(Y\) 是豪斯多夫空间。则重合集 \(C(f_0, f_1) = \{p \in X : f_0(p) = f_1(p)\}\) 是闭集。

Proof: For \(p \in X\) with \(f_0(p) \neq f_1(p)\) by the Hausdorffness of \(Y\), there are \(V_0 \in \mathcal{N}_{f_0(p)}\) and \(V_1 \in \mathcal{N}_{f_1(p)}\) such that \(V_0 \cap V_1 = \emptyset\). Let \(U = f_0^{-1}(V_0) \cap f_1^{-1}(V_1) \in \mathcal{N}_p\), and then for any \(q \in U_0 \cap U_1\), \(f_0(q) \in V_0\) is not equal to \(f_1(q) \in V_1\). Therefore the \(X \setminus C(f_0, f_1)\) is open. \(\blacksquare\)

12.4.1.5 Corollary: If \(A\) is a dense subset of a topological space \(X\) and \(Y\) is a Hausdorff space, then for any continuous map \(f: A \to Y\) its continuous extension \(g: X \to Y\) is unique if there is one.

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.1.5 推论：如果 \(A\) 是拓扑空间 \(X\) 的稠密子集，且 \(Y\) 是豪斯多夫空间，那么对于任意连续映射 \(f: A \to Y\)，其连续扩张 \(g: X \to Y\) 如果存在则是唯一的。

Proof: Let \(g_i: X \to Y\), \(i \in \{0, 1\}\), be continuous maps extending \(f: A \to Y\). The coincidence set \(C(g_0, g_1) = X\) since it is closed and contains the dense subset \(A\) of \(X\), and therefore \(g_0 = g_1\). \(\blacksquare\)

12.4.1.6 Theorem: Let \(\mathcal{F} = \{f_j: X \to Y_j\}_{j \in J}\) be a family of maps between topological spaces with the initial topology on \(X\) induced by \(\mathcal{F}\) which separates points in the sense that for any distinct \(p, q \in X\) there is \(i \in J\) with \(f_i(p) \neq f_i(q)\). If \(Y_j\) is \(\mathrm{T}_2\) for any \(j \in J\) then \(X\) is \(\mathrm{T}_2\). In particular, a subspace of a \(\mathrm{T}_2\) space is \(\mathrm{T}_2\); a product of \(\mathrm{T}_2\) spaces is \(\mathrm{T}_2\).

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.1.6 定理：设 \(\mathcal{F} = \{f_j: X \to Y_j\}_{j \in J}\) 为拓扑空间之间的映射族，\(X\) 上赋予由 \(\mathcal{F}\) 诱导的初始拓扑。该拓扑在以下意义上分离点：对于任意相异点 \(p, q \in X\)，存在 \(i \in J\) 使得 \(f_i(p) \neq f_i(q)\)。如果对于任意 \(j \in J\)，\(Y_j\) 都是 \(\mathrm{T}_2\) 空间，那么 \(X\) 也是 \(\mathrm{T}_2\) 空间。特别地，\(\mathrm{T}_2\) 空间的子空间是 \(\mathrm{T}_2\) 的；\(\mathrm{T}_2\) 空间的乘积是 \(\mathrm{T}_2\) 的。
[点击展开证明] Proof: Let \(p, q \in X\) be two distinct points. Since \(f_i(p) \neq f_i(q)\) and \(Y_i\) is \(\mathrm{T}_2\), there are \(U_i \in \mathcal{N}_{f_i(p)}\) and \(V_i \in \mathcal{N}_{f_i(q)}\) such that \(U_i \cap V_i = \emptyset\). By the definition of the initial topology on \(X\), we have \(f_i^{-1}(U_i) \in \mathcal{N}_p\), \(f_i^{-1}(V_i) \in \mathcal{N}_q\), and \(f_i^{-1}(U_i) \cap f_i^{-1}(V_i) = \emptyset\). Thus \(X\) is \(\mathrm{T}_2\). \(\blacksquare\)

12.4.2.1 Remark: Consider any topological space \(X\) and an equivalence relation \(\sim\) on \(X\). Denote the equivalence class of any \(x \in X\) by \([p]\). Then \(X / \sim\) is assumed to have the quotient topology with respect to the (quotient) map \(q: X \to X / \sim, p \mapsto [p]\).

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.2.1 备注：考虑任意拓扑空间 \(X\) 及其上的等价关系 \(\sim\)。将任意 \(x \in X\) 的等价类记为 \([p]\)。假设 \(X / \sim\) 赋予了关于（商）映射 \(q: X \to X / \sim, p \mapsto [p]\) 的商拓扑。

12.4.2.2 Proposition: Let \(q: X \to Q\) be a quotient map of topological spaces. Let \(Y\) be a topological space and \(f: X \to Y\) such that \(q(a) = q(b) \implies f(a) = f(b)\) holds for all \(a, b \in X\). Then there is a unique map \(\tilde{f}: Q \to Y\) such that \(f = \tilde{f} \circ q\).

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.2.2 命题：设 \(q: X \to Q\) 为拓扑空间的商映射。设 \(Y\) 为拓扑空间，\(f: X \to Y\) 满足对所有 \(a, b \in X\) 都有 \(q(a) = q(b) \implies f(a) = f(b)\)。则存在唯一的映射 \(\tilde{f}: Q \to Y\) 使得 \(f = \tilde{f} \circ q\)。
[点击展开证明] Proof: Define \(\tilde{f}: Q \to Y, q(x) \mapsto f(x)\). Then \(\tilde{f}\) is well-defined, since for all \(x, y \in X\) with \(q(y) = q(x)\), we have \(f(y) = f(x)\). This \(\tilde{f}\) satisfies \(f = \tilde{f} \circ q\). It is also continuous, because for any open \(U \subset Y\), \(q^{-1}(\tilde{f}^{-1}(U)) = f^{-1}(U)\) is open. By definition of the quotient topology on \(Q\), this means that \(\tilde{f}^{-1}(U)\) is open and therefore \(\tilde{f}\) is continuous. It is unique, since all \([p]\) should be sent to \(f(x)\) in order to get \(f = \tilde{f} \circ q\). \(\blacksquare\)

12.4.2.3 Corollary: Let \(X, Y, Z\) be topological spaces and \(f: X \to Y, g: X \to Z\) quotient maps such that for all \(a, b \in X\), \(f(a) = f(b) \iff g(a) = g(b)\). Then there is a unique homeomorphism \(h: Y \to Z\) such that \(g = h \circ f\). This \(h\) sends \(f(x)\) to \(g(x)\).

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.2.3 推论：设 \(X, Y, Z\) 为拓扑空间，\(f: X \to Y, g: X \to Z\) 为商映射，且满足对于所有 \(a, b \in X\)，\(f(a) = f(b) \iff g(a) = g(b)\)。则存在唯一的同胚 \(h: Y \to Z\) 使得 \(g = h \circ f\)。此 \(h\) 将 \(f(x)\) 映射到 \(g(x)\)。
[点击展开证明]

Proof: By proposition 2.4, there is unique \(\tilde{g}: Y \to Z\) such that \(g = \tilde{g} \circ f\). This \(\tilde{g}\) sends \(f(x)\) to \(g(x)\). By proposition 2.4, there is also a map \(\tilde{f}: Z \to Y\), \(g(x) \mapsto f(x)\). We can see that \(\tilde{f}\) is the inverse map of \(\tilde{g}\). Hence, \(\tilde{g}\) is a homeomorphism. For any quotient map \(f: X \to Y\) of topological spaces, one can define an equivalence relation \(\sim_f\) on \(X\) such that \(a \sim_f b \iff f(a) = f(b)\) for all \(a, b \in X\). Corollary 2.5 tells us that we could just as well say that \(Y\) is the unique quotient \(X / \sim_f\). \(\blacksquare\)

Since \(\mathrm{diag}(X)\) is the trivial equivalence relation, this corollary motivates the question whether the equivalence relations \(R\) on \(X\) such that \(X/R\) is Hausdorff are exactly the closed equivalence relations. We will see that only one implication holds.

12.4.2.4 Lemma: let \(R\) be an equivalence relation on \(X\) such that \(X/R\) is Hausdorff. Then \(R\) is closed as a subset of \(X \times X\).

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.2.4 引理：设 \(R\) 为 \(X\) 上的等价关系，使得 \(X/R\) 是豪斯多夫空间。则 \(R\) 作为 \(X \times X\) 的子集是闭的。
[点击展开证明]

Proof: Let \(q: X \to X/R\) be the quotient map and let \((a, b) \in R^\complement\). Then \(q(a) \neq q(b)\) and since \(X/R\) is Hausdorff, there are open \(U, V \subset X/R\) such that \(q(a) \in U, q(b) \in V\) and \(U \cap V = \emptyset\). By definition, \(q^{-1}(U)\) and \(q^{-1}(V)\) are open in \(X\), so \(q^{-1}(U) \times q^{-1}(V)\) is open in \(X \times X\). Now we have \((a, b) \in q^{-1}(U) \times q^{-1}(V)\) and because \(U \cap V = \emptyset\), we obtain \((q^{-1}(U) \times q^{-1}(V)) \cap R = \emptyset\). So for any \((a, b) \in R^\complement\), there is an open neighbourhood of \((a, b)\) that is a subset of \(R^\complement\). Therefore \(R^\complement\) is open and hence \(R\) is closed. \(\blacksquare\)

The following example shows that a closed equivalence relation on a topological space does not necessarily yield a quotient that is Hausdorff.

12.4.2.5 Example: Let \(X_1 = \mathbb{Q} \cap [0, 1]\), \(X_2 = ]0, 1]\) and \(X_3 = ([0, 1] \setminus \mathbb{Q}) \cup \{0\}\). Let \(X = X_1 \coprod X_2 \coprod X_3\). Let \(R\) be the equivalence relation on \(X\) given by \[(x, i) R (y, j) \iff (x = y \land (x, y > 0 \lor i = j)). \qquad (177)\]

12.4.2.6 Lemma: There is a topological space \(X\) and an equivalence relation \(R\) on \(X\) such that \(R\) is closed and \(X/R\) is not Hausdorff.

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.2.6 引理：存在拓扑空间 \(X\) 及其上的等价关系 \(R\)，使得 \(R\) 是闭的且 \(X/R\) 不是豪斯多夫空间。
[点击展开证明] Proof: Let \(((x, i), (y, j)) \in R^\complement\). If \(x \neq y\), we can find disjoint open neighbourhoods \(U \ni x\) and \(V \ni y\) in \([0, 1]\). For such \(U, V \subset [0, 1]\), it is true that \((U \cap X_i) \times (V \cap X_j) \cap R = \emptyset\) and \(((x, i), (y, j)) \in (U \cap X_i) \times (V \cap X_j)\). Now suppose \(x = y\). Then \(x = y = 0\) and \(i \neq j\) because \(((x, i), (y, j)) \in R^\complement\). Without loss of generality we can assume \(i = 1\) and \(j = 3\). Now observe that \(((0, 1), (0, 3)) \in X_1 \times X_3\) and \((X_1 \times X_3) \cap R = \emptyset\). Also notice that \(X_1 \times X_3\) is an open set. So every point in \(R^\complement\) has an open neighbourhood that is a subset of \(R^\complement\), so \(R^\complement\) is open. Therefore, \(R\) is closed. Let \(\pi: X \to X/R\) be the classification map. It will be shown that \(\pi(0, 1)\) and \(\pi(0, 3)\) cannot be separated by open sets, although \(\pi(0, 1) \neq \pi(0, 3)\). Let \(U, V\) be open neighbourhoods of \(\pi(0, 1)\) resp. \(\pi(0, 3)\). Then \(F = \pi^{-1}(U)\) and \(G = \pi^{-1}(V)\) are saturated open neighbourhoods of \((0, 1)\) and \((0, 3)\), respectively. So \(F\) contains \(([0, \delta_1[ \cap X_1) \times \{1\}\) for some \(\delta_1 > 0\) and \(G\) contains \(([0, \delta_3[ \cap X_3) \times \{3\}\) for some \(\delta_3 > 0\). Because they are saturated, \(F\) contains \(((0, \min(\delta_1, \delta_3)) \cap \mathbb{Q}) \times \{2\}\) and \(G\) contains \(((0, \min(\delta_1, \delta_3)) \setminus \mathbb{Q}) \times \{2\}\). We can see that any open set \(S \subset ]0, 1[\) containing \(((0, \min(\delta_1, \delta_3)) \cap \mathbb{Q})\) must contain some element of \(((0, \min(\delta_1, \delta_3)) \setminus \mathbb{Q})\), so \(F \cap G \neq \emptyset\). Therefore, \(U \cap V \neq \emptyset\), and \(X/R\) is not Hausdorff. \(\blacksquare\)

12.4.2.7 Definition: Let \(X \in \mathbf{Top}\) and let \(\sim_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) be the equivalence relation on \(X\) defined by \(p_0 \sim_{\mathrm{Haus}} p_1\) iff for any \(Y \in \mathbf{Haus}\) and continuous map \(f: X \to Y\) we have \(f(p_0) = f(p_1)\). Let \(H(X) = X / \sim_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) denote the quotient space, called the Hausdorff quotient of \(X\), and the classification map is denoted by \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}: X \to H(X)\), called the Hausdorff quotient map of \(X\).

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.2.7 定义：设 \(X \in \mathbf{Top}\)，并设 \(\sim_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) 为 \(X\) 上的等价关系，定义为 \(p_0 \sim_{\mathrm{Haus}} p_1\) 当且仅当对于任意 \(Y \in \mathbf{Haus}\) 及连续映射 \(f: X \to Y\) 都有 \(f(p_0) = f(p_1)\)。记商空间 \(H(X) = X / \sim_{\mathrm{Haus}}\)，称为 \(X\) 的豪斯多夫商 (Hausdorff quotient)，其分类映射记为 \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}: X \to H(X)\)，称为 \(X\) 的豪斯多夫商映射 (Hausdorff quotient map)。

12.4.2.8 Theorem: For any continuous map \(f: X \to Y\) from \(X \in \mathbf{Top}\) to \(Y \in \mathbf{Haus}\), there is a unique \(\tilde{f}: H(X) \to Y\) such that \(\tilde{f} \circ \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}} = f\).

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.2.8 定理：对于从 \(X \in \mathbf{Top}\) 到 \(Y \in \mathbf{Haus}\) 的任意连续映射 \(f: X \to Y\)，存在唯一的 \(\tilde{f}: H(X) \to Y\) 使得 \(\tilde{f} \circ \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}} = f\)。
[点击展开证明] Proof: For any Hausdorff space \(Y\) and any continuous maps \(f: X \to Y\), we have that \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}(p_0) = \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}(p_1)\) implies \(f(p_0) = f(p_1)\). Then, \(\tilde{f}: H(X) \to Y\), \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}(p) \mapsto f(p)\) is the required unique map. Now let \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}(p_0), \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}(p_1) \in H(X)\) with \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}(p_0) \neq \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}(p_1)\). Then there is a Hausdorff space \(Y\) and a continuous map \(f: X \to Y\) such that \(f(p_0) \neq f(p_1)\). Choose open neighbourhoods \(U \ni f(p_0)\) and \(V \ni f(p_1)\) such that \(U \cap V = \emptyset\). Then \(\tilde{f}^{-1}(U) \ni \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}(p_0)\) and \(\tilde{f}^{-1}(V) \ni \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}(p_1)\) are disjoint open sets, which proves that \(H(X)\) is Hausdorff. \(\blacksquare\)

12.4.2.9 Proposition: The equivalence relation \(\sim_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) from Definition 12.4.2.7 is the smallest equivalence relation \(S\) on \(X\) such that \(X/S\) is Hausdorff. In other words, \[\sim_{\mathrm{Haus}} = \bigcap \{ S \subset X \times X : S \text{ is an equivalence relation and } X/S \text{ is Hausdorff} \}. \qquad (178)\]

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.2.9 命题：定义 12.4.2.7 中的等价关系 \(\sim_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) 是 \(X\) 上使得 \(X/S\) 为豪斯多夫空间的最小等价关系 \(S\)。换句话说， \[\sim_{\mathrm{Haus}} = \bigcap \{ S \subset X \times X : S \text{ 是一个等价关系且 } X/S \text{ 是豪斯多夫空间} \}. \qquad (178)\]

Proof: On one hand, \(\sim_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) is itself an equivalence relation and \(H(X) = X / \sim_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) is Hausdorff. On the other hand, for any equivalence relation \(S\) on \(X\) with \(X/S\) Hausdorff, \(X \to X/S\) factors through \(X / \sim_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) and hence \(\sim_{\mathrm{Haus}} \subseteq S\). \(\blacksquare\)

12.4.2.14 Lemma: A topological space \(X\) is Hausdorff iff \(\overset{1}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) is the trivial equivalence relation.

12.4.2.15 Proposition: For any topological space \(X\), the inclusion \(\overset{1}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}} \subseteq \sim_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) holds.

12.4.2.16 Example: Let \(X\) be an infinite topological space with the cofinite topology, i.e. \(U \subset X\) is open iff \(U = \emptyset\) or \(U^\complement\) is finite. [点击展开翻译] 12.4.2.16 例：设 \(X\) 为赋予余有限拓扑的无限拓扑空间，即 \(U \subset X\) 是开集当且仅当 \(U = \emptyset\) 或 \(U^\complement\) 是有限的。那么不存在可以由开集分离的点，因此 \(\overset{1}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}} = X \times X\)。因为 \(\overset{1}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}} \subseteq \sim_{\mathrm{Haus}}\)，这意味着 \(\sim_{\mathrm{Haus}} = X \times X\) 且 \(H(X)\) 是一个单元素集。

12.4.2.17 Definition: Let \(X\) be a topological space. For each ordinal number \(\alpha\), define a topological space \(H^\alpha(X)\), an equivalence relation \(\overset{\alpha}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) on \(X\) and a map \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}^\alpha : X \to H^\alpha(X)\) in the following way: when \(\overset{\alpha}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) is defined, we let \(H^\alpha(X) = X / \overset{\alpha}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) with quotient map \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}^\alpha : X \to H^\alpha(X)\). With this convention, define \(\sim_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) recursively: \[\overset{0}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}} = \mathrm{diag}(X);\] \[\overset{\alpha+1}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}} = \left\{ (a, b) \in X \times X : \left( \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}^\alpha(a), \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}^\alpha(b) \right) \in \overset{1}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}, H^\alpha(X)} \right\} \text{ for all } \alpha \in \mathbf{Ord}; \qquad (179)\] \[\overset{\alpha}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}} = \bigcup_{\beta < \alpha} \overset{\beta}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}} \quad \text{if } \alpha \text{ is a limit.}\] Here \(\overset{1}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}, H^\alpha(X)}\) is the Hausdorff equivalence relation for \(H^\alpha(X)\).

Notice that \(\overset{\alpha}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}} \subseteq \overset{\beta}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) holds for any \(\alpha, \beta \in \mathbf{Ord}\) with \(\alpha < \beta\), provided that all \(\overset{\gamma}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) with \(\gamma < \beta\) are well-defined equivalence relations. So inductively it follows that \(\overset{\beta}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) is an equivalence relation for any limit \(\beta\), since it is a union of an ascending chain of equivalence relations. Therefore, all \(\overset{\alpha}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) are well-defined equivalence relations. Since \(\overset{0}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) is trivial, \(H^0(X) = X\) and hence \(H^1(X) = X / \overset{1}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}}\). For any two ordinals \(\alpha, \beta\) with \(\alpha < \beta\), \(\overset{\alpha}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}} \subseteq \overset{\beta}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) holds, so there is a unique map \(f_{\alpha, \beta} : H^\alpha(X) \to H^\beta(X)\) such that \(\pi^\beta_{\mathrm{Haus}} = f_{\alpha, \beta} \circ \pi^\alpha_{\mathrm{Haus}}\). Furthermore, \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, H^\alpha(X)} \circ \pi^\alpha_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) is a quotient map, so there is a unique homeomorphism \(g : H^{\alpha+1}(X) \to H^1(H^\alpha(X))\) such that \(g \circ \pi^{\alpha+1}_{\mathrm{Haus}} = \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, H^\alpha(X)} \circ \pi^\alpha_{\mathrm{Haus}}\).

12.4.2.18 Proposition: Let \(X\) be a topological space. For any \(\alpha \in \mathbf{Ord}\), \(\overset{\alpha}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}} \subseteq \sim_{\mathrm{Haus}}\).

12.4.2.19 Lemma: There is some ordinal \(\alpha\) such that \(\overset{\alpha}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}} = \overset{\alpha+1}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}}\).

12.4.2.21 Lemma: Let \(X\) be a topological space such that all equivalence classes of \(\overset{1}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) are finite and there are only finitely many equivalence classes with more than one element. Then \(H(X) = H^1(X)\).

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.2.21 引理：设 \(X\) 为一个拓扑空间，使得 \(\overset{1}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) 的所有等价类都是有限的，并且只有有限多个包含多于一个元素的等价类。则 \(H(X) = H^1(X)\)。
[点击展开证明] Proof: Let \(a, b \in X\) such that \(\pi^1_{\mathrm{Haus}}(a) \neq \pi^1_{\mathrm{Haus}}(b)\). For any \(p \in X\), let \([p]_1\) be the equivalence class of \(p\) with respect to \(\overset{1}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}}\). Let \(Y\) be the union of all equivalence classes \([p]_1\) with more than one element such that \(\{a, b\} \cap [p]_1 = \emptyset\). For each \(q \in Y\) and each \(p \in [a]_1\), there is an open neighbourhood \(U_{p,q}\) of \(p\) such that \(q \notin U_{p,q}\), because \((p, q) \notin \overline{\mathrm{diag}(X)}\). Now \(U = \bigcup_{p \in [a]_1} \bigcap_{q \in Y} U_{p,q}\) is an open neighbourhood of \([a]_1\) such that \(Y \cap U = \emptyset\). Similarly, there is an open neighbourhood \(V\) of \(B\) such that \(Y \cap V = \emptyset\). Each pair of elements \(p_1, q_1\) with \(p_1 \in [a]_1\) and \(q_1 \in [b]_1\) can be separated by open neighbourhoods \(U_{p_1, q_1}\) and \(V_{p_1, q_1}\), because \((p_1, q_1) \notin \overline{\mathrm{diag}(X)}\). Therefore, \([a]_1\) and \([b]_1\) can be separated by open neighbourhoods \(S := \bigcap_{q_1 \in [b]_1} \bigcup_{p_1 \in [a]_1} U_{p_1, q_1}\) and \(T := \bigcup_{q_1 \in [b]_1} \bigcap_{p_1 \in [a]_1} V_{p_1, q_1}\). Therefore, \(\pi^1_{\mathrm{Haus}}(a)\) and \(\pi^1_{\mathrm{Haus}}(b)\) are separated by the open neighbourhoods \(\pi^1_{\mathrm{Haus}}(U \cap S)\) and \(\pi^1_{\mathrm{Haus}}(V \cap T)\). Hence \(H^1(X)\) is Hausdorff and \(H(X) = H^1(X)\). \(\blacksquare\)

12.4.2.22 Corollary: If \(X\) is a finite topological space, then \(H(X) = H^1(X)\).

So \(H(X) = H^1(X) \cong \mathbb{R}\).
[点击展开翻译] 12.4.2.23 例：设 \(X = (\mathbb{R} \times \{0, 1\}) / \sim\)，其中 \(\sim\) 是 \(\mathbb{R} \times \{0, 1\}\) 上的一个等价关系，满足对于所有 \(x \in \mathbb{R} \setminus \{0\}\) 都有 \((x, 0) \sim (x, 1)\)。那么 \(X\) 可以被看作是带有双原点的实直线，并且 \(\{(x, 0), (x, 1)\}\) 是 \(\overset{1}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) 中唯一一个包含多于一个元素的等价类。所以 \(H(X) = H^1(X) \cong \mathbb{R}\)。
In general, \(H(X) = H^1(X)\) does not hold.
[点击展开翻译] 一般而言，\(H(X) = H^1(X)\) 并不成立。
12.4.2.24 Example: Let \(X = \mathbb{N} \cup \{\infty\}\) with the topology given by a basis that consists of all sets of the form \(\{2n - 1\}, \{2n - 1, 2n, 2n + 1\}\), or \([2n - 1, \infty]\), with \(n \in \mathbb{N}\). Since \((n, n + 1) \in \overline{\mathrm{diag}(X)}\) and \((n, \infty) \notin \overline{\mathrm{diag}(X)}\) for each \(n \in \mathbb{N}\), the equivalence classes of \(\overset{1}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) are \(\mathbb{N}\) and \(\{\infty\}\). However, the latter is not open in \(X\), so 1 and \(\infty\) cannot be separated by saturated open neighbourhoods (with respect to \(\pi^1_{\mathrm{Haus}}\)). Therefore, \(\pi^1_{\mathrm{Haus}}(1)\) and \(\pi^1_{\mathrm{Haus}}(\infty)\) cannot be separated by open sets, whereas \(\pi^1_{\mathrm{Haus}}(1) \neq \pi^1_{\mathrm{Haus}}(\infty)\). Hence \(H^1(X)\) is not Hausdorff and \(H^1(X) \neq H(X)\). In this case, \(H^2(X) = H(X)\) does hold since \(H^1(X)\) is finite.
[点击展开翻译] 12.4.2.24 例：设 \(X = \mathbb{N} \cup \{\infty\}\)，其拓扑由形如 \(\{2n - 1\}, \{2n - 1, 2n, 2n + 1\}\) 或 \([2n - 1, \infty]\) 的所有集合构成的基给出，其中 \(n \in \mathbb{N}\)。因为对于每个 \(n \in \mathbb{N}\) 都有 \((n, n + 1) \in \overline{\mathrm{diag}(X)}\) 且 \((n, \infty) \notin \overline{\mathrm{diag}(X)}\)，所以 \(\overset{1}{\sim}_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) 的等价类为 \(\mathbb{N}\) 和 \(\{\infty\}\)。然而，后者在 \(X\) 中不是开集，因此 1 和 \(\infty\) 无法由饱和的开邻域（关于 \(\pi^1_{\mathrm{Haus}}\)）分离。因此，\(\pi^1_{\mathrm{Haus}}(1)\) 和 \(\pi^1_{\mathrm{Haus}}(\infty)\) 无法由开集分离，尽管 \(\pi^1_{\mathrm{Haus}}(1) \neq \pi^1_{\mathrm{Haus}}(\infty)\)。故 \(H^1(X)\) 不是豪斯多夫空间且 \(H^1(X) \neq H(X)\)。在这种情况下，\(H^2(X) = H(X)\) 确实成立，因为 \(H^1(X)\) 是有限的。

12.4.3 The category Haus. | \(\mathbf{Haus}\) 范畴。

One may form the category of all small Hausdorff spaces or of all small compact Hausdorff spaces.
[点击展开翻译] 我们可以构造所有小豪斯多夫空间的范畴，或者所有小紧豪斯多夫空间的范畴。
12.4.3.1 Example: By \(\mathbf{Haus}\) we denote the category with objects all small Hausdorff spaces with morphisms all continuous functions.
[点击展开翻译] 12.4.3.1 例：我们用 \(\mathbf{Haus}\) 表示以所有小豪斯多夫空间为对象、以所有连续函数为态射的范畴。
The category \(\mathbf{Haus}\) is a large but locally small category. The category \(\mathbf{Haus}\) is a reflective and full sub-category of \(\mathbf{Top}\). The fullness of \(\mathbf{Haus}\) in \(\mathbf{Top}\) is trivial. We have to prove the reflectivity.
[点击展开翻译] 范畴 \(\mathbf{Haus}\) 是一个大但局部小的范畴。 范畴 \(\mathbf{Haus}\) 是 \(\mathbf{Top}\) 的一个反射且完全的子范畴。\(\mathbf{Haus}\) 在 \(\mathbf{Top}\) 中的完全性是平凡的。我们需要证明其反射性。

12.4.3.2 Proposition: For any \(X \in \mathbf{Top}\) the Hausdorff quotient map \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) is a reflection from \(X \in \mathbf{Top}\) to \(H(X) \in \mathbf{Haus}\).

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.3.2 命题：对于任意 \(X \in \mathbf{Top}\)，豪斯多夫商映射 \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) 是从 \(X \in \mathbf{Top}\) 到 \(H(X) \in \mathbf{Haus}\) 的一个反射。
[点击展开证明] Proof: Let \(Y\) be a Hausdorff space and \(f: X \to Y\) a continuous map. If \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}(p_0) = \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}(p_1)\), then, by definition of the equivalence relation \(\sim_{\mathrm{Haus}}\), \(f(p_0) = f(p_1)\). Consequently, any \(f: X \to Y\) is compatible with \(\sim_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) and by consequence, there exists a unique function \(\tilde{f}: X / \sim_{\mathrm{Haus}} \to Y\) such that \(\tilde{f} = f \circ \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}\). We have to show that \(\tilde{f}\) is a continuous map and that \(X / \sim_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) is a Hausdorff space. Recall that \(U\) is an open subset of \(X / \sim_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) iff \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}^{-1}(U)\) is an open subset of \(X\). Let \(V\) be an open subset of \(Y\), then \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}^{-1}(\tilde{f}^{-1}(V)) = f^{-1}(V)\) is an open set of \(X\). We deduce that \(\tilde{f}^{-1}(U)\) is an open set of \(X / \sim_{\mathrm{Haus}}\) and as a consequence, \(\tilde{f}\) is continuous. If \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}(p_0) \neq \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}(p_1)\), then there is a Hausdorff space \(Y\) and a continuous map \(f: X \to Y\) such that \(f(p_0) \neq f(p_1)\). By the Hausdorffness, there are two disjoint open subsets \(V_0\) and \(V_1\) of \(Y\) such that \(f(p_0) \in V_0\) and \(f(p_1) \in V_1\). It implies that \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}(p_0) \in \tilde{f}^{-1}(V_0)\) and \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}}(p_1) \in \tilde{f}^{-1}(V_1)\). The subsets \(\tilde{f}^{-1}(V_0)\) and \(\tilde{f}^{-1}(V_1)\) are clearly disjoint open subsets of \(X / \sim_{\mathrm{Haus}}\), which is thus a Hausdorff space. \(\blacksquare\)

12.4.3.3 Proposition: In \(\mathbf{Haus}\) the monomorphisms are the continuous injections, the epimorphisms are the continuous maps with dense image, and the bimorphisms are continuous injections with dense image.

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.3.3 命题：在 \(\mathbf{Haus}\) 中，单态射是连续单射，满态射是具有稠密像的连续映射，双态射是具有稠密像的连续单射。

Cancelling \(f\) on the left we have \(c_{p_0} = c_{p_1}\), which implies that \(p_0 = p_1\) and \(f\) is an injection. If \(f\) is injective, then let \(h_0, h_1: Z \to X\) be continuous maps such that \(f \circ h_0 = f \circ h_1\). For any \(r \in Z\), we have \(f(h_0(r)) = f(h_1(r))\), and by injectivity we have \(h_0(r) = h_1(r)\), this implies that \(h_0 = h_1\). Then, \(f\) is left-cancellable and is a monomorphism. 12.4.4 The Hausdorff quotient functor. | 豪斯多夫商函子。

In this section, the general construction of \(H(X)\) from a topological space \(X\) will be extended to a functor \(H: \mathbf{Top} \to \mathbf{Haus}\).
[点击展开翻译] 在本节中，从拓扑空间 \(X\) 构造 \(H(X)\) 的一般构造将被推广为一个函子 \(H: \mathbf{Top} \to \mathbf{Haus}\)。

12.4.4.1 Definition: The functor \(H: \mathbf{Top} \to \mathbf{Haus}\) sends for any \(X \in \mathbf{Top}\) to its Hausdorff quotient \(H(X)\), and any \(f: X \to Y\) in \(\mathbf{Top}\), to \(H(f)\) be the unique continuous map \(H(X) \to H(Y)\) such that \(H(f) \circ \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X} = \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, Y} \circ f\). This map exists and is unique, since \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, Y} \circ f: X \to H(Y)\) is a map from \(X\) to a Hausdorff space.

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.4.1 定义：函子 \(H: \mathbf{Top} \to \mathbf{Haus}\) 将任意 \(X \in \mathbf{Top}\) 映射到其豪斯多夫商 \(H(X)\)，并将 \(\mathbf{Top}\) 中的任意 \(f: X \to Y\) 映射为 \(H(f)\)，即满足 \(H(f) \circ \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X} = \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, Y} \circ f\) 的唯一连续映射 \(H(X) \to H(Y)\)。该映射存在且唯一，因为 \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, Y} \circ f: X \to H(Y)\) 是从 \(X\) 到豪斯多夫空间的映射。
It needs to be checked that this \(H\) is indeed a functor. It is clear that it sends identity morphisms to identity morphisms, since \(\mathrm{id}_{H(X)}\) is the unique map \(h: H(X) \to H(X)\) such that \(h \circ \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}} = \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}} \circ \mathrm{id}_X\) for all \(X \in \mathbf{Top}\). So \(H(\mathrm{id}_X) = \mathrm{id}_{H(X)}\). Composition is also preserved by \(H\). Let \(X, Y, Z \in \mathbf{Top}\) with morphisms \(f: X \to Y, g: Y \to Z\) in \(\mathbf{Top}\). Then \((H(g) \circ H(f)) \circ \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X} = H(g) \circ (\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, Y} \circ f) = \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, Z} \circ (g \circ f)\). Hence, \(H(g) \circ H(f)\) is the unique map \(h: H(X) \to H(Y)\) such that \(h \circ \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X} = \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, Z} \circ (g \circ f)\). So \(H(g) \circ H(f) = H(g \circ f)\). The following main question that will be answered is whether the Hausdorff Functor preserves homotopy.
[点击展开翻译] 我们需要验证这个 \(H\) 确实是一个函子。显然它将恒等态射映射为恒等态射，因为 \(\mathrm{id}_{H(X)}\) 是使得对于所有 \(X \in \mathbf{Top}\) 都有 \(h \circ \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}} = \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}} \circ \mathrm{id}_X\) 的唯一映射 \(h: H(X) \to H(X)\)。所以 \(H(\mathrm{id}_X) = \mathrm{id}_{H(X)}\)。\(H\) 也保持复合运算。设 \(X, Y, Z \in \mathbf{Top}\) 且在 \(\mathbf{Top}\) 中有态射 \(f: X \to Y, g: Y \to Z\)。那么 \((H(g) \circ H(f)) \circ \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X} = H(g) \circ (\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, Y} \circ f) = \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, Z} \circ (g \circ f)\)。因此，\(H(g) \circ H(f)\) 是满足 \(h \circ \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X} = \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, Z} \circ (g \circ f)\) 的唯一映射 \(h: H(X) \to H(Y)\)。所以 \(H(g) \circ H(f) = H(g \circ f)\)。 接下来的一个主要问题是豪斯多夫函子是否保持同伦，我们将会回答这个问题。

12.4.4.2 Theorem: Let \(X, Y\) be topological spaces. There is a continuous bijection \[H(X \times Y) \to H(X) \times H(Y),\] \[\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X \times Y}(p, q) \mapsto (\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X}(p), \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, Y}(q)). \qquad (181)\]

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.4.2 定理：设 \(X, Y\) 为拓扑空间。存在一个连续双射 \[H(X \times Y) \to H(X) \times H(Y),\] \[\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X \times Y}(p, q) \mapsto (\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X}(p), \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, Y}(q)). \qquad (181)\]
[点击展开证明] Proof: Consider the following maps: \[\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X} \circ \pi_1: X \times Y \to H(X), \quad (p, q) \mapsto \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X}(p)\] \[\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, Y} \circ \pi_2: X \times Y \to H(Y), \quad (p, q) \mapsto \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, Y}(q). \qquad (182)\] By the universal property of the product \(H(X) \times H(Y)\), this induces a map \(N: X \times Y \to H(X) \times H(Y), (p, q) \mapsto (\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X}(x), \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, Y}(y))\). Since \(H(X) \times H(Y)\) is Hausdorff as a product of two Hausdorff spaces, this map factors through \(H(X \times Y)\), so there is a map \(\tilde{N}: H(X \times Y) \to H(X) \times H(Y)\) that sends \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X \times Y}(p, q)\) to \((\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X}(p), \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, Y}(q))\). Because \(N\) is surjective, \(\tilde{N}\) is also surjective. Now let \(p_0, p_1 \in X, q_0, q_1 \in Y\) be such that \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X}(p_0) = \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X}(p_1)\) and \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, Y}(q_0) = \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, Y}(q_1)\) and let \(f: X \times Y \to Z\) with \(Z\) Hausdorff. Consider these maps \[g: X \to X \times Y, a \mapsto (a, q_0)\] \[h: Y \to X \times Y, b \mapsto (p_1, b). \qquad (183)\] It follows that \[ \begin{aligned} f(p_0, q_0) &= (f \circ g)(p_0) = (f \circ g)(p_1) = f(p_1, q_0) \\ &= (f \circ h)(q_0) = (f \circ h)(q_1) = f(p_1, q_1). \qquad (184) \end{aligned} \] So \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X \times Y}(p_0, q_0) = \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X \times Y}(p_1, q_1)\). Therefore, \(\tilde{N}\) is injective and hence a bijection. \(\blacksquare\)

12.4.4.3 Corollary: Let \(X, Y\) be topological spaces such that \(H(X \times Y)\) is compact. Then \(\tilde{N}: H(X \times Y) \to H(X) \times H(Y)\), \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X \times Y}(p, q) \mapsto (\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X}(p), \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, Y}(q))\) is a homeomorphism.

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.4.3 推论：设 \(X, Y\) 为拓扑空间且使得 \(H(X \times Y)\) 为紧致空间。那么 \(\tilde{N}: H(X \times Y) \to H(X) \times H(Y)\), \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X \times Y}(p, q) \mapsto (\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X}(p), \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, Y}(q))\) 是一个同胚。

Proof: Being a continuous bijection from a compact space to a Hausdorff space, \(\tilde{N}\) is a homeomorphism. \(\blacksquare\)

12.4.4.4 Corollary: Let \(X, Y\) be compact topological spaces. Then \(\tilde{N}: H(X \times Y) \to H(X) \times H(Y)\), \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X \times Y}(p, q) \mapsto (\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X}(x), \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, Y}(y))\) is a homeomorphism.

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.4.4 推论：设 \(X, Y\) 为紧拓扑空间。那么 \(\tilde{N}: H(X \times Y) \to H(X) \times H(Y)\), \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X \times Y}(p, q) \mapsto (\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X}(x), \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, Y}(y))\) 是一个同胚。

Proof: The space \(H(X \times Y) = \pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X \times Y}(X \times Y)\) is compact, because it is a continuous image of the compact space \(X \times Y\). Therefore, \(\tilde{N}\) is a homeomorphism. \(\blacksquare\)

In the rest of this section we denote the unit interval \([0, 1]\) by \(I\). The following lemma can be found in a more general form in [6].
[点击展开翻译] 在本节的其余部分，我们将单位区间 \([0, 1]\) 记为 \(I\)。下面的引理可以在 [6] 中找到其更一般的形式。

12.4.4.5 Lemma: Let \(f: X \to Y\) be a quotient map of topological spaces. Then \(F: X \times I \to Y \times I\), \((p, t) \mapsto (f(p), t)\) is a quotient map.

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.4.5 引理：设 \(f: X \to Y\) 为拓扑空间的商映射。那么 \(F: X \times I \to Y \times I\), \((p, t) \mapsto (f(p), t)\) 也是一个商映射。
[点击展开证明] Proof: Let \(U \subseteq X \times I\) be a saturated open set and let \((p, t) \in U\). Since \(U\) is open, there is a basic open neighbourhood \(V \times W \subseteq U\) of \((p, t)\). But there is also a closed interval \(W^\prime \subseteq W\) that is a neighbourhood of \(t\). Define the set \(V^\prime = \{ v \in X : \{v\} \times W^\prime \subseteq U \}\). Let \(a \in V^\prime\) and \(b \in X\) with \(f(a) = f(b)\). For any \(w \in W^\prime\), we have \(F(b, w) = F(a, w)\), so \((b, w) \in U\) because \(U\) is saturated. Therefore we get \(\{b\} \times W^\prime \subseteq U\) and hence \(b \in V^\prime\), and \(V^\prime\) is saturated with respect to \(f\). Let \(a \in V^\prime\). Because \(\{a\} \times W^\prime \subseteq U\), there is an open neighbourhood \(A_w \times B_w\) of \((a, w)\) for each \(w \in W^\prime\). The set of all \(B_w\) now covers \(W^\prime\). Since \(W^\prime\) is compact, it has a finite subcover \(\{ B_j : j \in J \}\) for some finite \(J \subseteq W^\prime\). We see that \(\left(\bigcap_{j \in J} A_j\right) \times W^\prime \subset \left(\bigcap_{j \in J} A_j\right) \times \left(\bigcup_{j \in J} B_j\right) \subseteq U\) holds. So, \(v \in V^\prime\) holds for all \(v \in \bigcap_{j \in J} A_j\). Hence \(a\) has an open neighbourhood \(\bigcap_{j \in J} A_j \subseteq V^\prime\), so \(V^\prime\) is open. Now observe that \(V^\prime \times W^{\prime \circ} \subseteq U\) is a saturated open neighbourhood of \((p, t)\). Thus \(U\) is a union of basic open sets as \(U = \bigcup_{u \in U} (A_u \times B_u)\), where \(A_u \times B_u\) is an open neighbourhood of \(u\) and all \(A_u\) are saturated. Therefore \(F(U) = F\left(\bigcup_{u \in U} (A_u \times B_u)\right) = \bigcup_{u \in U} (f(A_u) \times B_u)\), which is open. So \(F\) sends saturated open sets to open sets and is hence a quotient map. \(\blacksquare\)

12.4.4.6 Theorem: Let \(X\) be a topological space. The canonical map \(C: H(X \times I) \to H(X) \times I\), \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X \times I}(p, q) \mapsto (\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X}(p), q)\) is a homeomorphism.

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.4.6 定理：设 \(X\) 为一个拓扑空间。典范映射 \(C: H(X \times I) \to H(X) \times I\), \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X \times I}(p, q) \mapsto (\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X}(p), q)\) 是一个同胚。

Proof: Since \(I\) is Hausdorff, \(H(I) = I\) holds. So, \(C\) is a well-defined bijective continuous map. Because of the lemma, \(X \times I \to H(X) \times I, (p, t) \mapsto (\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X}(p), t)\) is a quotient map. By the corollary, \(C\) is a homeomorphism. \(\blacksquare\)

We prove that the Hausdorff Functor preserves homotopy. This can be stated otherwise by introducing the homotopy category and saying that \(H\) induces a functor in this category.
[点击展开翻译] 我们证明豪斯多夫函子保持同伦。换句话说，通过引入同伦范畴，我们可以说 \(H\) 在该范畴中诱导了一个函子。

12.4.4.7 Proposition: The functor \(H\) induces a functor in the homotopy category. That is, whenever \(f, g: X \to Y\) are homotopic maps of topological spaces, then \(H(f)\) and \(H(g)\) are also homotopic.

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.4.7 命题：函子 \(H\) 在同伦范畴中诱导了一个函子。即，每当 \(f, g: X \to Y\) 是拓扑空间的同伦映射时，\(H(f)\) 和 \(H(g)\) 也是同伦的。
[点击展开证明] Proof: Let \(F: X \times I \to Y\) be a homotopy between \(f\) and \(g\). It easily follows that \(H(F): H(X \times I) \to H(Y)\) is a homotopy between \(H(f)\) and \(H(g)\), after identifying \(H(X \times I)\) with \(H(X) \times I\). Now let \([f]\) denote the homotopy class of any morphism \(f\) in \(\mathbf{Top}\). Since \([H(f)] = [H(g)]\) whenever \(f \simeq g\), we can define a functor \(H_h\) in the homotopy category by \(H_h(X) = H(X)\) for all \(X \in \mathbf{Top}\) and \(H_h([f]) = [H(f)]\) for all morphisms \(f\) in \(\mathbf{Top}\). \(\blacksquare\)

12.4.4.8 Corollary: A topological space \(X\) is Hausdorff iff \(X\) and \(H(X)\) are homeomorphic.

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.4.8 推论：一个拓扑空间 \(X\) 是豪斯多夫空间，当且仅当 \(X\) 与 \(H(X)\) 同胚。

Proof: As \(\mathbf{Haus}\) is a full subcategory of \(\mathbf{Top}\), one can use the proposition I.4.20 of [adamek]. \(\blacksquare\)

The epimorphisms of \(\mathbf{Haus}\) are image-dense morphisms.
[点击展开翻译] \(\mathbf{Haus}\) 的满态射是像稠密的态射。

12.4.4.9 Proposition: Let \(f: X \to Y\) be an epimorphism in \(\mathbf{Haus}\), then \(\overline{f(X)} = Y\).

[点击展开翻译] 12.4.4.9 命题：设 \(f: X \to Y\) 为 \(\mathbf{Haus}\) 中的一个满态射，则 \(\overline{f(X)} = Y\)。
[点击展开证明] Proof: Let \(\pi: Y \to Y / \overline{f(X)}\) be the quotient map and \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X}: Y / \overline{f(X)} \to H(Y / \overline{f(X)})\) be the Hausdorff quotient map. We have \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X} \circ \pi \circ f\) is constant and as \(f\) is an epimorphism of \(\mathbf{Haus}\), then \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X} \circ \pi\) is constant. So, either \(\pi\) is constant or \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X}\) is constant. If \(\pi\) is constant, then \(Y = \overline{f(X)}\). Suppose that \(Y \neq \overline{f(X)}\) and \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X}\) is constant. Let \(q \in Y \setminus \overline{f(X)}\). As \(\overline{f(X)}\) is a closed subset of \(Y\), then there exists an open subset \(V\) of \(Y\) such that \(q \in V \subset Y \setminus \overline{f(X)}\). By definition of \(\pi\), for \(q_0\) and \(q_1\) in \(V\), \(\pi(q_0) = \pi(q_1)\) iff \(q_0 = q_1\). It implies that \(\pi^{-1}(\pi(V)) = V\) and \(\pi(V)\) is a Hausdorff open subset of \(Y / \overline{f(A)}\). We deduce that \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X} \circ \pi(V)\) has at least one point which is different from \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X} \circ \pi\left(\overline{f(A)}\right)\). Consequently, \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X}\) takes at least two different values; this fact is contradictory with \(\pi_{\mathrm{Haus}, X}\) constant. \(\blacksquare\)

12.6.1 Counterexamples of implications. | 蕴含关系的反例。

12.6.1.1 Proposition: Let \(\mathcal{T}\) be the topology on \(\mathbb{R}\) generated by \(\mathcal{T}_{\mathbb{R}} \cup \mathcal{T}_{\text{coc}}\). Then \((\mathbb{R}, \mathcal{T})\) is Hausdorff but not regular.

[点击展开翻译] 12.6.1.1 命题：设 \(\mathcal{T}\) 为由 \(\mathcal{T}_{\mathbb{R}} \cup \mathcal{T}_{\text{coc}}\) 生成的 \(\mathbb{R}\) 上的拓扑。则 \((\mathbb{R}, \mathcal{T})\) 是豪斯多夫空间但不是正则空间。
[点击展开证明] Proof: Since \(\mathcal{T}\) is finer than a Hausdorff topology \(\mathcal{T}_{\mathbb{R}}\), it is itself Hausdorff. We leave as an exercise that \(U \subseteq \mathbb{R}\) is open under the topology \(\mathcal{T}\) iff it is written as \(U = V \cap W\) for \(V \in \mathcal{T}_{\mathbb{R}}\) and \(W \in \mathcal{T}_{\text{coc}}\). Now \(B = \mathbb{Q}\) is closed whose only open neighborhood is \(\mathbb{R}\), and then it violates the assumption of the \(\mathbf{T}_3\) axiom with any \(p \in \mathbb{R} \setminus \mathbb{Q}\). \(\blacksquare\)

12.6.1.2 Example: Let \(H = \mathbb{R} \times ]0, \infty[\) be the upper half plane and \(\overline{H} = \mathbb{R} \times [0, \infty[\) be the closed upper half plane. Let \[\mathcal{B} = \mathcal{B}_H \cup \{B_{(x,y)}(y) \cup \{(x,0)\} : (x,y) \in H\}. \qquad (198)\] The Moore plane is \((\overline{H}, \mathcal{T}(\mathcal{B}))\).

12.6.1.3 Proposition: The Moore plane is regular but not normal.

[点击展开翻译] 12.6.1.3 命题：摩尔平面是正则的但不是正规的。
[点击展开证明] Proof: The Moore plane \((\overline{H}, \mathcal{T}_{\text{Moore}})\) satisfies the \(\mathbf{T}_1\) axiom since every point is closed. For the \(\mathbf{T}_3\) axiom it remains to check that for any \(x \in \mathbb{R}\) and \(r > 0\) the set \(B_{(x,r)}(r) \cup \{(x, 0)\}\) contains a closed neighborhood \(D_{(x,\frac{r}{2})}(\frac{r}{2}) = \mathrm{Cl}(B_{(x,\frac{r}{2})}(\frac{r}{2}) \cup \{(x,0)\})\) of \((x, 0)\). Thus the Moore plane is regular. We claim that for any \(A \subseteq \mathbb{R}\), the set \(A \times \{0\}\) is closed in \((\overline{H}, \mathcal{T}_{\text{Moore}})\). In fact, taking the union of open sets \(H\) and \(B_{(x,1)}(1) \cup \{(x, 0)\}\) for \(x \in \mathbb{R} \setminus A\) we obtain the complement of \(A \times \{0\}\) in \(\overline{H}\). Note that \(Q = \mathbb{Q} \times (\mathbb{Q} \cap ]0, \infty[)\) is dense in \((\overline{H}, \mathcal{T}_{\text{Moore}})\), and if assuming the \(\mathbf{T}_4\) axiom we have the injection \[\mathrm{Map}(\mathbb{R} \times \{0\}; [0, 1]) \to C((\mathbb{R} \times \{0\}, \mathcal{T}_{\text{disc}}); [0, 1]) \to C((\overline{H}, \mathcal{T}_{\text{Moore}}); [0, 1]) \to \mathrm{Map}(Q; [0, 1]) \qquad (199)\] where \(\mathrm{Map}(\cdot; \cdot)\) is the set of maps and \(C(\cdot; \cdot)\) is the set of continuous maps. The first arrow is the identity since every map from a discrete space is continuous, the second arrow is by Tietze extension theorem, the third arrow is injective by the denseness of \(Q\). However comparing cardinalities \[|\mathrm{Map}(\mathbb{R} \times \{0\}; [0, 1])| = |[0, 1]| ^{|\mathbb{R} \times \{0\}|} = |\mathbb{R}|^{|\mathbb{R}|},\] \[|\mathrm{Map}(Q; [0, 1])| = |[0, 1]| ^{|Q|} = |\mathbb{R}|^{|\mathbb{N}|}. \qquad (200)\] Then \(|\mathbb{R}|^{|\mathbb{R}|} > |\mathbb{R}|^{|\mathbb{N}|}\) would lead to a contradiction and then the Moore plane is not normal. To see the inequality above one could use \(|\mathbb{R}|^{|\mathbb{R}|} \geq 2^{|\mathbb{R}|} > |\mathbb{R}| = |\mathbb{R}|^{|\mathbb{N}|}\) but actually \(|\mathbb{R}|^{|\mathbb{R}|} = 2^{|\mathbb{R}|}\) since \(|\mathbb{R}|^{|\mathbb{R}|} \leq (2^{|\mathbb{R}|})^{|\mathbb{R}|} = 2^{|\mathbb{R}| \cdot |\mathbb{R}|} = 2^{|\mathbb{R}|}\). \(\blacksquare\)

12.6.1.4 Example: Let \(\overline{H}_* = \overline{H} \cup \{\infty\}\) and let \[\mathcal{T}_{\text{Moore}}^* = \mathcal{T}_{\text{Moore}} \cup \{(\mathbb{R} \setminus F) \times \{0\} \cup \{\infty\} : F \subseteq \mathbb{R}, |F| < \infty\}. \qquad (201)\] Then \((\overline{H}_*, \mathcal{T}_{\text{Moore}}^*)\) is a topological space.

12.6.1.5 Proposition: The space \((\overline{H}_*, \mathcal{T}_{\text{Moore}}^*)\) is normal but contains the Moore space as a subspace.

[点击展开翻译] 12.6.1.5 命题：空间 \((\overline{H}_*, \mathcal{T}_{\text{Moore}}^*)\) 是正规的，但包含摩尔空间作为其子空间。

12.6.1.6 Definition: A subset of a topological space \(X\) is \(G_\delta\) if it is the intersection of countably many open sets; it is \(F_\sigma\) if it is the union of countably many closed sets.

[点击展开翻译] 12.6.1.6 定义：拓扑空间 \(X\) 的一个子集如果是可数个开集的交，则称为 \(G_\delta\) 集；如果是可数个闭集的并，则称为 \(F_\sigma\) 集。

Every open subset is trivially a \(G_\delta\)-subset, while every closed subset is a \(F_\sigma\)-subset.

12.6.1.7 Theorem: In a \(\mathbf{T}_4\) space any two disjoint \(G_\delta\) closed subsets are precisely separated by a function. In particular, a \(\mathbf{T}_4\) space is perfectly \(\mathbf{T}_4\) iff all closed subsets are \(G_\delta\).

[点击展开翻译] 12.6.1.7 定理：在 \(\mathbf{T}_4\) 空间中，任意两个不相交的 \(G_\delta\) 闭子集由函数精确分离。特别地，一个 \(\mathbf{T}_4\) 空间是完美 \(\mathbf{T}_4\) 空间，当且仅当所有闭子集都是 \(G_\delta\) 集。
[点击展开证明] Proof: We observe that \(\mathbb{R}\) has the property that all closed subsets are \(G_\delta\) (equivalently, all open subsets are \(F_\sigma\)). Then for any continuous \(f: X \to \mathbb{R}\), the \(f\)-saturated closed subsets of \(X\) are \(G_\delta\). In particular, if \(f\) precisely separates two closed subsets \(A\) and \(B\) of \(X\), then \(A = f^{-1}(0)\) and \(B = f^{-1}(0)\) must be \(G_\delta\). Now suppose \(A\) and \(B\) are two \(G_\delta\) closed subsets of \(X\); then there are decreasing families \((V_j)_{j \in \mathbb{N}}\) and \((W_j)_{j \in \mathbb{N}}\) of open subsets of \(X\) such that \(A = \bigcap_{j=1}^\infty V_j\) and \(B = \bigcap_{j=1}^\infty W_j\). By the above lemma we achieve a tuple \(([0, 1], (U_s)_{s \in [0, 1]}, (F_s)_{s \in [0, 1]})\) satisfying certain monotonicity properties. The amended tuple \[\left([0, 1], (\tilde{U}_s \cap V_j)_{s \in [0, 1]}, (\tilde{F}_s)_{s \in [0, 1]}\right), \quad \tilde{U}_s = U_s \cap V_{\lfloor \frac{1}{s} \rfloor}, \tilde{F}_s = F_s \cap W_{\lfloor \frac{1}{s} \rfloor}^\complement \qquad (202)\] satisfies the same properties, and the Urysohn function \(\tilde{f}\) is generated from the amended tuple. \(\blacksquare\)

12.6.2 Hereditariness of separation axioms. | 分离公理的遗传性。

12.6.2.1 Theorem: The \(\mathbf{T}_0\), \(\mathbf{T}_1\), \(\mathbf{T}_2\), and \(\mathbf{T}_3\) axioms are hereditary. The \(\mathbf{T}_4\) axiom is closed-hereditary but not hereditary.

[点击展开翻译] 12.6.2.1 定理：\(\mathbf{T}_0\)、\(\mathbf{T}_1\)、\(\mathbf{T}_2\) 和 \(\mathbf{T}_3\) 公理是遗传的。\(\mathbf{T}_4\) 公理是闭遗传的但不是遗传的。
[点击展开证明] Proof: The \(\mathbf{T}_0\), \(\mathbf{T}_1\), and \(\mathbf{T}_2\) axioms are hereditary since a neighborhood restricted to a subspace is still a neighborhood. Let \(A\) be a subspace of \(X\). For the \(\mathbf{T}_3\) axiom, if \(p \in A\) and closed \(B \subseteq A\) are disjoint, then there is a closed \(B_1 \subseteq X\) such that \(B_1 \cap A = B\). In particular, \(p \notin B_1\), and if \(X\) is \(\mathbf{T}_3\), then \(p\) and \(B_1\) are separated by neighborhoods \(U_1\) and \(V_1\) in \(X\), and then \(p\) and \(B\) are separated by neighborhoods \(U_1 \cap A\) and \(V_1 \cap B\) in \(A\). This argument does not work for \(\mathbf{T}_4\) as if \(C\) and \(B\) are disjoint closed subset of \(A\) we may not find closed subsets \(C_1\) and \(B_1\) of \(X\) with \(C_1 \cap A = C\) and \(B_1 \cap A = B\) that are still disjoint; it works when \(A\) is closed in \(X\). In this case, the \(\mathbf{T}_4\) axiom could be applied directly to \(C\) and \(B\) in \(X\). For counterexample of the hereditary of \(\mathbf{T}_4\) see the Moore plane with an extra point. \(\blacksquare\)

12.6.2.2 Theorem: Two nonempty topological spaces are \(\mathrm{T}_2\) iff their disjoint union is and iff their product is.

[点击展开翻译] 12.6.2.2 定理：两个非空拓扑空间是 \(\mathrm{T}_2\) 空间，当且仅当它们的不交并是 \(\mathrm{T}_2\) 空间，且当且仅当它们的乘积是 \(\mathrm{T}_2\) 空间。

12.6.2.3 Theorem: A product of regular spaces is regular, but a product of normal spaces is not necessarily normal.

[点击展开翻译] 12.6.2.3 定理：正则空间的乘积是正则的，但正规空间的乘积不一定是正规的。

Proof: For the counterexamples see the product of two real lines with the lower limit topology (Exercise 12.6.3.6). \(\blacksquare\)

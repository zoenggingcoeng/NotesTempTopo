17.1 Distances between subsets. | 子集之间的距离

17.1.0.1 Definition: Let \((X, d)\) be a premetric space. Let \(p \in X\), and \(A, B \subseteq X\). The distance \(d(p, A)\) between \(p\) and \(A\) and the distance \(d(A, B)\) between \(A\) and \(B\) are \[d(p, B) = \inf_{q \in B} d(p, q),\] \[d(A, q) = \inf_{p \in A} d(p, q), \qquad (249)\] \[d(A, B) = \inf_{p \in A} d(p, B) = \inf_{p \in A, q \in B} d(p, q).\]
[点击展开翻译] 17.1.0.1 定义： 设 \((X, d)\) 是一个前度量空间。设 \(p \in X\)，且 \(A, B \subseteq X\)。\(p\) 与 \(A\) 之间的距离 \(d(p, A)\) 以及 \(A\) 与 \(B\) 之间的距离 \(d(A, B)\) 为 \[d(p, B) = \inf_{q \in B} d(p, q),\] \[d(A, q) = \inf_{p \in A} d(p, q), \qquad (249)\] \[d(A, B) = \inf_{p \in A} d(p, B) = \inf_{p \in A, q \in B} d(p, q).\]
17.1.0.2 Proposition: The distance between a point and a set satisfies a version of the triangle inequality \[d(p, A) \le d(p, q) + d(q, A). \qquad (250)\] The distance between sets is a (symmetric) premetric on \(\mathcal{P}(X)\) when \(d\) is a (symmetric) premetric on \(X\).
[点击展开翻译] 17.1.0.2 命题： 点与集合之间的距离满足一种形式的三角不等式 \[d(p, A) \le d(p, q) + d(q, A). \qquad (250)\] 当 \(d\) 是 \(X\) 上的（对称）前度量时，集合之间的距离是幂集 \(\mathcal{P}(X)\) 上的一个（对称）前度量。
17.1.0.3 Proposition: In a metric space \((X, d)\), for \(p \in X\) and \(A \subseteq X\), the distance \(d(p, A) = 0\) iff \(p \in \text{Cl}(A)\).
[点击展开翻译] 17.1.0.3 命题： 在度量空间 \((X, d)\) 中，对于 \(p \in X\) 和 \(A \subseteq X\)，距离 \(d(p, A) = 0\) 当且仅当 \(p \in \text{Cl}(A)\)。
17.1.0.4 Proposition: In a metric space \((X, d)\), if \((A, \mathcal{T}_A)\) is a compact subspace and \(B\) is a closed subset, then \(d(A, B) = 0\) iff \(A \cap B \ne \emptyset\).
[点击展开翻译] 17.1.0.4 命题： 在度量空间 \((X, d)\) 中，如果 \((A, \mathcal{T}_A)\) 是紧子空间且 \(B\) 是闭子集，那么 \(d(A, B) = 0\) 当且仅当 \(A \cap B \ne \emptyset\)。

17.2 Hausdorff distances. | Hausdorff 距离

17.2.0.1 Definition: Let \((X, d)\) be a premetric space with \(\mathcal{CB}\) be the collection of nonempty, closed, and bounded subsets. Let \(A, B \in \mathcal{CB}\). The Hausdorff distance \(d_{\text{H}}(A, B)\) between \(A\) and \(B\) is \[d_{\text{H}}(A, B) = \max \left( \sup_{p \in A} d(p, B), \sup_{q \in B} d(A, q) \right). \qquad (251)\]
[点击展开翻译] 17.2.0.1 定义： 设 \((X, d)\) 是一个前度量空间，\(\mathcal{CB}\) 为非空有界闭子集的收集。设 \(A, B \in \mathcal{CB}\)。\(A\) 与 \(B\) 之间的Hausdorff 距离 (Hausdorff distance) \(d_{\text{H}}(A, B)\) 为 \[d_{\text{H}}(A, B) = \max \left( \sup_{p \in A} d(p, B), \sup_{q \in B} d(A, q) \right). \qquad (251)\]
17.2.0.2 Theorem: In a metric space \((X, d)\), the Hausdorff distance \(d_{\text{H}}\) is a metric on \(\mathcal{CB}\).
[点击展开翻译] 17.2.0.2 定理： 在度量空间 \((X, d)\) 中，Hausdorff 距离 \(d_{\text{H}}\) 是 \(\mathcal{CB}\) 上的一个度量。
[点击展开证明] Proof: The finiteness and other properties of \(d_{\text{H}}\) are left as exercises. For \(A, B \in \mathcal{CB}\) let \(r(A, B) = \sup_{p \in A} d(p, B)\). We deduce the triangle inequality. Let \(A, B, C \in \mathcal{CB}\). For any \(p \in A\), since \(d(p, C) \le d(p, q) + d(q, C) \le d(p, q) + r(B, C)\), by the arbitrarity of \(q \in B\), we obtain \(d(p, C) \le d(p, B) + r(B, C)\). Taking the supremum over \(p \in A\) we have \(r(A, C) \le r(A, B) + r(B, C)\), which is sufficient for the triangle inequality. \(\blacksquare\)

17.3 The Gromov–Hausdorff distance. | Gromov-Hausdorff 距离

17.3.0.1 Definition: Let \((X, d_X)\) and \((Y, d_Y)\) be metric spaces. The Gromov–Hausdorff distance between \((X, d_X)\) and \((Y, d_Y)\) is \[d_{\text{GH}}((X, d_X), (Y, d_Y)) = \inf_{i, j} d_{\text{H}}(i(X), j(Y)) \qquad (252)\] where the infimum is taken among all metric spaces \((Z, d_Z)\) and isometries \(i: (X, d_X) \to (Z, d_Z), j: (Y, d_Y) \to (Z, d_Z)\).
[点击展开翻译] 17.3.0.1 定义： 设 \((X, d_X)\) 和 \((Y, d_Y)\) 是度量空间。\((X, d_X)\) 与 \((Y, d_Y)\) 之间的Gromov-Hausdorff 距离 (Gromov-Hausdorff distance) 为 \[d_{\text{GH}}((X, d_X), (Y, d_Y)) = \inf_{i, j} d_{\text{H}}(i(X), j(Y)) \qquad (252)\] 其中下确界取遍所有度量空间 \((Z, d_Z)\) 以及等距映射 \(i: (X, d_X) \to (Z, d_Z), j: (Y, d_Y) \to (Z, d_Z)\)。

It turns out that, to define the Gromov–Hausdorff distance, it suffices to consider only metric spaces of the form \((X \sqcup Y, \rho)\), where \(\rho\) extends \(d_X\) and \(d_Y\), called an admissible metric, and the set of all admissible metrics is denoted by \(D(X, Y)\).

17.3.0.2 Lemma: Let \((X, d_X)\) and \((Y, d_Y)\) be metric spaces. Then \[d_{\text{GH}}((X, d_X), (Y, d_Y)) = \inf_{\rho} \rho_{\text{H}}(X, Y) \qquad (253)\] where \(\rho_{\text{H}}\) is the Hausdorff distance in \((X \sqcup Y, \rho)\).
[点击展开翻译] 17.3.0.2 引理： 设 \((X, d_X)\) 和 \((Y, d_Y)\) 是度量空间。那么 \[d_{\text{GH}}((X, d_X), (Y, d_Y)) = \inf_{\rho} \rho_{\text{H}}(X, Y) \qquad (253)\] 其中 \(\rho_{\text{H}}\) 是 \((X \sqcup Y, \rho)\) 中的 Hausdorff 距离。
17.3.0.3 Theorem: The Gromov–Hausdorff distance \(d_{\text{GH}}\) is a metric among the isometric isomorphism classes of compact metric spaces. Moreover, the metric is complete.
[点击展开翻译] 17.3.0.3 定理： Gromov-Hausdorff 距离 \(d_{\text{GH}}\) 是紧度量空间的等距同构类之间的一个度量。此外，该度量是完备的。
[点击展开证明] Proof: Let \((X, d_X)\), \((Y, d_Y)\), and \((Z, d_Z)\) be compact metric spaces. We prove the triangle inequality \[d_{\text{GH}}((X, d_X), (Z, d_Z)) \le d_{\text{GH}}((X, d_X), (Y, d_Y)) + d_{\text{GH}}((Y, d_Y), (Z, d_Z)) \qquad (254)\] Let \(\mu \in D(X, Y)\) and \(\nu \in D(Y, Z)\) be admissible metrics. Consider the metric space \(A = X \sqcup Y \sqcup Z\) with metric \(\rho\) extending both \(\mu\) and \(\nu\), and therefore \[\rho(p, r) = \inf_{q \in Y} \{\mu(p, q) + \nu(q, r)\}, \quad \forall p \in X, r \in Z. \qquad (255)\] We leave to the reader that \(\rho\) is a metric. Then the inclusions \(i: (X \sqcup Y, \mu) \to (A, \rho)\) and \(j: (Y \sqcup Z, \nu) \to (A, \rho)\) are isometries. \[d_{\text{GH}}((X, d_X), (Z, d_Z)) \le \rho_{\text{H}}(X, Z) \le \mu_{\text{H}}(X, Y) + \nu_{\text{H}}(Y, Z). \qquad (256)\] Taking the infimums over all \(\mu \in D(X, Y)\) and \(\nu \in D(Y, Z)\) we obtain the triangle inequality. \(\blacksquare\)

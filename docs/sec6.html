6.1 Topological subspaces | 拓扑子空间

6.1.1 Topological subspaces | 拓扑子空间

6.1.1.1 Definition: Let \(A\) be a subset of a topological space \((X, \mathcal{T}_X)\). Then the subspace topology is defined as \(\mathcal{T}_A = \{U \cap A : U \in \mathcal{T}_X\}\), and \((A, \mathcal{T}_A)\) is called a topological subspace of \((X, \mathcal{T}_X)\).
[点击展开翻译] 定义： 设 \(A\) 是拓扑空间 \((X, \mathcal{T}_X)\) 的一个子集。则 子空间拓扑 定义为 \(\mathcal{T}_A = \{U \cap A : U \in \mathcal{T}_X\}\)，且称 \((A, \mathcal{T}_A)\) 为 \((X, \mathcal{T}_X)\) 的一个 拓扑子空间。
6.1.1.2 Proposition: The subcollection \(\mathcal{T}_A \subseteq \mathcal{P}(A)\) is indeed a topology on \(A\).
[点击展开翻译] 命题： 子集合 \(\mathcal{T}_A \subseteq \mathcal{P}(A)\) 确实是 \(A\) 上的一个拓扑。
6.1.1.3 Remark: The default topology on a subset \(A\) of a topological space \(X\) is the subspace topology.
[点击展开翻译] 备注： 拓扑空间 \(X\) 的子集 \(A\) 上默认的拓扑即为子空间拓扑。
6.1.1.4 Proposition: A subset \(B\) of \(A\) is open iff there is an open subset \(U\) of \(X\) with \(B = U \cap A\); it is closed iff there is a closed subset \(C\) of \(X\) with \(B = C \cap A\). In particular, if \(A\) is open then open subsets of \(A\) are subsets of \(A\) that are open in \(X\); and if \(A\) is closed then closed subsets of \(A\) are subsets of \(A\) that are closed in \(X\).
[点击展开翻译] 命题： \(A\) 的子集 \(B\) 是开集当且仅当存在 \(X\) 的开子集 \(U\) 使得 \(B = U \cap A\)；它是闭集当且仅当存在 \(X\) 的闭子集 \(C\) 使得 \(B = C \cap A\)。特别地，如果 \(A\) 是开集，则 \(A\) 的开子集也是 \(X\) 中的开集；如果 \(A\) 是闭集，则 \(A\) 的闭子集也是 \(X\) 中的闭集。

6.1.1.5 Proposition:

  1. A topological subspace of a Hausdorff space is Hausdorff.
  2. A topological subspace of a separable space is separable.
  3. A topological subspace of a first countable space is first countable.
  4. A topological subspace of a second countable space is second countable.
[点击展开翻译]

命题：

  1. Hausdorff 空间的拓扑子空间是 Hausdorff 的。
  2. 可分空间的拓扑子空间是可分的。
  3. 第一可数空间的拓扑子空间是第一可数的。
  4. 第二可数空间的拓扑子空间是第二可数的。

6.1.2 Restriction and gluing | 限制与粘贴

6.1.2.1 Proposition: If \(f: X \to Y\) is a continuous map on a topological space \(X\), then for any subset \(A\) of \(X\) the restriction \(f|_A: A \to Y\), \(p \mapsto f(p)\) is also continuous.

[点击展开翻译] 命题： 设 \(f: X \to Y\) 是拓扑空间 \(X\) 上的连续映射，则对于 \(X\) 的任何子集 \(A\)，其限制映射 \(f|_A: A \to Y\), \(p \mapsto f(p)\) 也是连续的。

Gluing lemma: Let \(X\) be a topological space with a family of subsets \((A_i)_{i \in I}\) that covers \(X\). If \(f: X \to Y\) is a map such that \(f|_{A_i}: A_i \to Y\) is continuous for any \(i \in I\), then \(f\) is continuous assuming either \(A_i\) are all open in \(X\), or \(I\) is finite and \(A_i\) are all closed in \(X\).

[点击展开翻译] 粘贴引理： 设 \(X\) 为一拓扑空间，\((A_i)_{i \in I}\) 为覆盖 \(X\) 的一个子集族。若映射 \(f: X \to Y\) 满足对任意 \(i \in I\)，\(f|_{A_i}: A_i \to Y\) 均连续，则在以下任一假设下 \(f\) 也是连续的：所有 \(A_i\) 均在 \(X\) 中开，或者 \(I\) 为有限集且所有 \(A_i\) 均在 \(X\) 中闭。

6.2 Compact spaces | 紧空间

6.2.1 Compact intervals | 紧区间

6.2.1.1 Definition: An subset \(A\) of \(\mathbb{R}\) is bounded iff there is an \(M > 0\) such that \(|x| < M\) for any \(x \in A\).

[点击展开翻译] 定义： \(\mathbb{R}\) 的子集 \(A\) 是有界的，当且仅当存在 \(M > 0\)，使得对任何 \(x \in A\) 均有 \(|x| < M\)。

We classify intervals by boundedness.

[点击展开翻译] 我们根据有界性对区间进行分类。
  • bounded
    • empty: \(\emptyset\),
    • degenerate: \(\{a\}\),
    • proper: \(]a, b[\), \([a, b]\), \(]a, b]\), \([a, b[\);
  • left-bounded and right-unbounded: \(]a, \infty[\), \([a, \infty[\);
  • left-unbounded and right-bounded: \(]-\infty, b[\), \(]-\infty, b]\);
  • whole set: \(\mathbb{R}\).
[点击展开翻译]
  • 有界
    • 空集：\(\emptyset\)，
    • 退化区间：\(\{a\}\)，
    • 固有区间：\(]a, b[\)，\([a, b]\)，\(]a, b]\)，\([a, b[\)；
  • 左有界且右无界：\(]a, \infty[\)，\([a, \infty[\)；
  • 左无界且右有界：\(]-\infty, b[\)，\(]-\infty, b]\)；
  • 全集：\(\mathbb{R}\)。
There are special properites of bounded closed intervals, which we recall from Mathematical Analysis.
[点击展开翻译] 有界闭区间具有特殊的性质，我们在此复习数学分析中的相关内容。

6.2.1.2 Extremal value: A continuous function \(f: I \to \mathbb{R}\) on a bounded closed interval \(I\) attains both the maximum and the minimum values; that is, there are \(x, y \in I\) such that \(f(x) \le f(z) \le f(y)\) for any \(z \in I\).

[点击展开翻译] 极值定理： 定义在有界闭区间 \(I\) 上的连续函数 \(f: I \to \mathbb{R}\) 能够取到最大值和最小值；即存在 \(x, y \in I\)，使得对任何 \(z \in I\) 均有 \(f(x) \le f(z) \le f(y)\)。

6.2.1.3 Bolzano–Weierstrass: A sequence \((x_n)_{n \in \mathbb{N}}\) in a bounded closed interval \(I\) has a subsequence \((x_{n_k})_{k \in \mathbb{N}}\) convergent to some \(c \in I\).

[点击展开翻译] Bolzano–Weierstrass 定理： 有界闭区间 \(I\) 中的序列 \((x_n)_{n \in \mathbb{N}}\) 拥有一个收敛于某个 \(c \in I\) 的子序列 \((x_{n_k})_{k \in \mathbb{N}}\)。

6.2.1.4 Heine–Borel: An open cover \((U_n)_{n \in I}\) of a bounded closed interval \(I\) has a finite subcover.

[点击展开翻译] Heine–Borel 定理： 有界闭区间 \(I\) 的开覆盖 \((U_n)_{n \in I}\) 拥有一个有限子覆盖。

6.2.1.5 Cantor’s intersection: A nested sequence \(F_1 \supseteq F_2 \supseteq \cdots\) of closed subsets of a bounded closed interval \(I\) has nonempty intersection \(\bigcap_{n=1}^\infty F_n\).

[点击展开翻译] Cantor 区间套定理： 有界闭区间 \(I\) 的闭子集构成的嵌套序列 \(F_1 \supseteq F_2 \supseteq \cdots\) 具有非空交集 \(\bigcap_{n=1}^\infty F_n\)。

6.2.1.6 Remark: Bounded closed intervals and finite sets in \(\mathbb{R}\) share similar topological properties, which we will call compactness.

  1. A continuous function \(f: A \to \mathbb{R}\) on a finite set \(A\) is any function on \(A\) attains both the maximum and the minimum values.
  2. A sequence \((x_n)_{n \in \mathbb{N}}\) in a finite set \(A\) has a constant subsequence \((x_{n_k})_{k \in \mathbb{N}}\) with some value \(c \in A\) by the pigeonhole principle.
  3. An open cover \((U_n)_{n \in I}\) of a finite set \(A\) has a finite subcover by choosing an open set covering each element of \(A\).
  4. A nested sequence \(F_1 \supseteq F_2 \supseteq \cdots\) of closed subsets of a finite set \(A\) is simply a nested sequence of finite subsets of \(A\), whose cardinalities are non-increasing positive integers, and must stabilize, and therefore, there is a \(N \in \mathbb{N}\) such that \(F_n = F_N\) for \(n \ge N\) and then the sequence has nonempty intersection \(\bigcap_{n=1}^\infty F_n = F_N\). Hence, we say that compactness is a generalization of finiteness.
[点击展开翻译]

备注： \(\mathbb{R}\) 中的有界闭区间与有限集具有相似的拓扑性质，我们将这种性质称为“紧性”。

  1. 有限集 \(A\) 上的连续函数 \(f: A \to \mathbb{R}\)（即 \(A\) 上的任意函数）都能取到最大值和最小值。
  2. 根据鸽巢原理，有限集 \(A\) 中的序列 \((x_n)_{n \in \mathbb{N}}\) 拥有一个值为 \(c \in A\) 的常数子序列 \((x_{n_k})_{k \in \mathbb{N}}\)。
  3. 通过为 \(A\) 中的每个元素选择一个包含它的开集，有限集 \(A\) 的开覆盖 \((U_n)_{n \in I}\) 必有有限子覆盖。
  4. 有限集 \(A\) 的闭子集构成的嵌套序列 \(F_1 \supseteq F_2 \supseteq \cdots\) 本质上是 \(A\) 的有限子集构成的嵌套序列，其基数是非增正整数且必然稳定。因此，存在 \(N \in \mathbb{N}\) 使得当 \(n \ge N\) 时 \(F_n = F_N\)，从而该序列具有非空交集 \(\bigcap_{n=1}^\infty F_n = F_N\)。 因此，我们说紧性是有限性的一种推广。
The properties Theorem 6.2.1.2, Theorem 6.2.1.3, Theorem 6.2.1.4, and Theorem 6.2.1.5 imply one another. We prove Heine–Borel implies extremal value problem for example.
[点击展开翻译] 定理 6.2.1.2、定理 6.2.1.3、定理 6.2.1.4 和定理 6.2.1.5 的性质是互蕴的。例如，我们证明 Heine–Borel 性质可以推导出极值问题。

6.2.1.7 Proposition: If a subset \(A\) of \(\mathbb{R}\) possesses the Heine–Borel property then the extremal value theorem holds for functions on \(A\).

[点击展开翻译] 命题： 若 \(\mathbb{R}\) 的子集 \(A\) 具有 Heine–Borel 性质，则极值定理在 \(A\) 上的函数中成立。
[点击展开证明] Proof: Let \(f: I \to \mathbb{R}\) be a continuous function. Let \((q_n)_{n \in \mathbb{N}}\) be an increasing sequence in \(\mathbb{R}\) such that \(\lim_{n \to \infty} q_n = \sup_I f\). Let \(U_n = \{x \in I : f(x) < q_n\}\) which is open in \(I\). If either \(f\) is unbounded above or \(f\) never takes \(\sup_I f\) then \(\{U_n\}_{n \in \mathbb{N}}\) is an open cover of \(I\) and by Heine–Borel has a subcover \(\{U_n\}_{n=1}^N\) for some \(N \in \mathbb{N}\). This implies that \(f(x) < q_n < \sup_I f\) and contradicts to the definition of the supremum. analogously, \(f\) is bounded below and attains \(\inf_I f\). The extremal value theorem hold for \(f\). ■
These does not hold for subspaces on non-open or unbounded subsets of \(\mathbb{R}\), such as \(\mathbb{R}\), \([0, 1[\) and \(]0, 1[\).
[点击展开翻译] 这些性质对于 \(\mathbb{R}\) 的非开或无界子空间不成立，例如 \(\mathbb{R}\)、\([0, 1[\) 和 \(]0, 1[\)。

6.2.2 Compactness | 紧性

Consider the discrete (free) topology functor \(F: \text{Set} \to \text{Top}\), which is the left inverse of the forgetful functor \(U: \text{Top} \to \text{Set}\). Then \(F(X)\) is compact iff \(X\) is finite.
[点击展开翻译]

考虑离散（自由）拓扑函子 \(F: \text{Set} \to \text{Top}\)，它是遗忘函子 \(U: \text{Top} \to \text{Set}\) 的左逆。则 \(F(X)\) 是紧的当且仅当 \(X\) 是有限的。

一个离散拓扑空间是紧致的当且仅当它的点数是有限的

6.2.2.1 Definition: A topological space \(X\) is compact iff every open cover of \(X\) has a finite subcover.

[点击展开翻译] 定义： 一个拓扑空间 \(X\) 是紧的，当且仅当 \(X\) 的每个开覆盖都具有有限子覆盖。

6.2.2.2 Proposition: Let \(A\) be a subset of \(\mathbb{R}\). The topological subspace on \(A\) is compact iff \(A\) is bounded and closed.

[点击展开翻译] 命题： 设 \(A\) 为 \(\mathbb{R}\) 的子集。\(A\) 上的拓扑子空间是紧的，当且仅当 \(A\) 是有界闭集。

6.2.2.3 Remark: The finiteness/compactness is so important as to get global information from local information, called the local-to-global principal. For example, the extremal value theorem: local (pointwise) bounded (continuity) + compact = global bounded!

[点击展开翻译] 备注： 有限性/紧性之所以如此重要，是因为它能让我们从局部信息获取整体信息，这被称为“局部-整体原理（local-to-global principal）”。例如，极值定理可以理解为：局部（逐点）有界性（连续性）+ 紧性 = 整体有界性！

6.3 Connected spaces | 连通空间

6.3.1 Connectedness | 连通性

6.3.1.1 Definition: A topological space \(X\) is connected iff for any open subsets \(U_1\) and \(U_2\) such that \(U_1 \sqcup U_2 = X\), exactly one of the two subsets is the empty set.

[点击展开翻译] 定义： 一个拓扑空间 \(X\) 是连通的，当且仅当对于任何满足 \(U_1 \sqcup U_2 = X\) 的开子集 \(U_1\) 和 \(U_2\)，这两个子集中恰好有一个是空集。

6.3.1.2 Proposition: A topological space \(X\) is connected iff it has exactly two clopen subsets.

[点击展开翻译] 命题： 一个拓扑空间 \(X\) 是连通的，当且仅当它恰好有两个既开又闭的子集（clopen subsets）。

6.3.1.3 Connectedness of star unions: Let \(X\) be a topological space with a family of connected subspaces \(\{A_i\}_{i \in I}\). If \(\bigcap_{i \in I} A_i \neq \emptyset\), then \(\bigcup_{i \in I} A_i\) is a connected subspace of \(X\).

[点击展开翻译] 星状并的连通性： 设 \(X\) 为一个拓扑空间，\(\{A_i\}_{i \in I}\) 为其一组连通子空间。若 \(\bigcap_{i \in I} A_i \neq \emptyset\)，则 \(\bigcup_{i \in I} A_i\) 是 \(X\) 的连通子空间。
[点击展开证明] Proof: Let \(U\) be a clopen subset of \(A = \bigcup_{i \in I} A_i\). Then for each \(i \in I, U \cap A_i\) is clopen in \(A_i\). Since \(A_i\) is connected for each \(i \in I\), there is a \(J \subseteq I\) such that \(i \in J\) iff \(A_i \subseteq U\) and \(i \in I \setminus J\) iff \(A_i \subseteq U^c\). However, for any \(i_1 \in J\) and \(i_2 \in I \setminus J\), we have \(A_{i_1} \cap A_{i_2} \subseteq U \cap U^c = \emptyset\). Therefore, if \(\bigcap_{i \in I} A_i \neq \emptyset\), then \(J \times (I \setminus J) = \emptyset\) and then either \(U = \emptyset\) or \(U = A\), and \(A\) is connected. ■

6.3.1.4 Connectedness of chain unions: Let \(X\) be a topological space with a sequence of connected subspaces \(\{A_n\}_{n \in \mathbb{N}}\). If \(A_n \cap A_{n+1} \neq \emptyset\) for any \(n \in \mathbb{N}\), then \(A = \bigcup_{n \in \mathbb{N}} A_n\) is a connected subspace of \(X\).

[点击展开翻译] 链状并的连通性： 设 \(X\) 为一个拓扑空间，\(\{A_n\}_{n \in \mathbb{N}}\) 为其一列连通子空间。若对于任何 \(n \in \mathbb{N}\) 均有 \(A_n \cap A_{n+1} \neq \emptyset\)，则 \(A = \bigcup_{n \in \mathbb{N}} A_n\) 是 \(X\) 的连通子空间。

6.3.1.5 Proposition: For any point \(p\) of a topological space \(X\) there is a unique maximal connected subspace \(C_p\) of \(X\) containing \(p\).

[点击展开翻译] 命题： 对于拓扑空间 \(X\) 的任意一点 \(p\)，存在唯一的包含 \(p\) 的极大连通子空间 \(C_p\)。

6.3.1.6 Definition: Let \(p\) be a point of a topological space \(X\). The maximal connected subspace \(C_p\) of \(X\) containing \(p\) is called the component of \(p\) in \(X\).

[点击展开翻译] 定义： 设 \(p\) 为拓扑空间 \(X\) 的一个点。包含 \(p\) 的极大连通子空间 \(C_p\) 称为 \(p\) 在 \(X\) 中的连通分支（component）。

6.3.1.7 Definition: A topological space \(X\) is totally disconnected if every point constitutes a component.

[点击展开翻译] 定义： 若一个拓扑空间 \(X\) 的每个点都构成一个连通分支，则称该空间是完全不连通（totally disconnected）的。

6.3.2 Connected subspaces of the real line | 实直线的连通子空间

6.3.2.1 Theorem: A subspace \(A\) of \(\mathbb{R}\) is connected iff \(A\) is a nonempty interval.

[点击展开翻译] 定理： \(\mathbb{R}\) 的子空间 \(A\) 是连通的，当且仅当 \(A\) 是一个非空区间。
[点击展开证明] Proof: It is an exercise for the reader that a nonempty interval is connected. If \(A \subset \mathbb{R}\) yields a connected subspace, then it must be nonempty. For any \(z \in \mathbb{R} \setminus A\), since \(]-\infty, z[ \cap A\) and \(]z, \infty[ \cap A\) separates \(A\), either \(A \subseteq ]-\infty, z[\) or \(A \subseteq ]z, \infty[\). In particular, for any \(x, y \in A\) and \(z \notin A\), we must have \(x\) and \(y\) lie on the same side of \(z\) and therefore \(A\) is an interval. ■

6.4 Examples of subspaces of \(\mathbb{R}\) | \(\mathbb{R}\) 的子空间示例

6.4.1 The spaces of rational and irrational numbers | 有理数与无理数空间

The rationals \(\mathbb{Q}\) and irrationals \(\mathbb{R} \setminus \mathbb{Q}\) has subspace topology structures inherited from the standard topology on \(\mathbb{R}\).
[点击展开翻译] 有理数集 \(\mathbb{Q}\) 和无理数集 \(\mathbb{R} \setminus \mathbb{Q}\) 具有从 \(\mathbb{R}\) 的标准拓扑中继承的子空间拓扑结构。

6.4.1.1 Proposition: The rational numbers \(\mathbb{Q}\) as a topological subspace of \(\mathbb{R}\) is neither compact nor connected; it is in fact totally disconnected.

[点击展开翻译] 命题： 作为 \(\mathbb{R}\) 的拓扑子空间，有理数集 \(\mathbb{Q}\) 既不是紧的也不是连通的；事实上，它是完全不连通的。

6.4.1.2 Proposition: The irrational numbers \(\mathbb{R} \setminus \mathbb{Q}\) as a topological subspace of \(\mathbb{R}\) is neither compact nor connected; it is in fact totally disconnected.

[点击展开翻译] 命题： 作为 \(\mathbb{R}\) 的拓扑子空间，无理数集 \(\mathbb{R} \setminus \mathbb{Q}\) 既不是紧的也不是连通的；事实上，它是完全不连通的。

6.4.2 The Cantor space | Cantor 空间

6.4.2.1 Definition: The Cantor space is the topological subspace of \(\mathbb{R}\) on the Cantor set \(C\).

[点击展开翻译] 定义： Cantor 空间是 \(\mathbb{R}\) 在 Cantor 集 \(C\) 上的拓扑子空间。

6.4.2.2 Proposition: The Cantor space \(C\) are compact and totally disconnected.

[点击展开翻译] 命题： Cantor 空间 \(C\) 是紧的且完全不连通的。
